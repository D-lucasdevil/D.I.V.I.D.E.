***File: LD-043***  

**Codename: “The Crimson Judgement”**  
**Designation: Unknown Weapon Artifact / Reality-Reactive Blade**  
**Threat Level: [REDACTED]**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 5+**  
**Containment Status: Secured — Maximum Restriction**

**Overview**  

LD-043 is a katana-type bladed weapon composed of an unknown metallic compound displaying a constant deep crimson coloration. The blade is embedded with multiple small crystalline formations of a similar red hue along its spine and guard.  

The object was first discovered following a [REDACTED] Pulse Event, though the pulse classification and origin have been sealed by DIVIDE High Command.  

Initial attempts to classify LD-043 resulted in multiple catastrophic containment failures.  

Due to the nature of these events, large portions of the official documentation remain permanently redacted.  

**Physical Description**  

LD-043 displays the following observable characteristics:  

Approximate length: 103 cm  

Curved katana-style blade  

Surface coloration: dark crimson metallic  

Embedded crystalline nodes along blade spine  

Crystals emit faint low-frequency resonance  

Material analysis attempts have failed due to extreme instability when samples are removed.  

The weapon shows no signs of corrosion, wear, or damage, even after exposure to extreme heat, ballistic impact, and corrosive chemical agents.  

**Activation Behavior**  

When an individual attempts to wield LD-043, the following phenomenon occurs:  

Subject grips the weapon.  

The crystals begin emitting a high-pitched harmonic resonance.  
 
If the subject is deemed “not worthy”, catastrophic biological restructuring begins.  

**Observed effects:**  

Subject’s body splits along the vertical axis  

Flesh and bone crystallize into deep red mineral formations  

Transformation occurs slowly while the subject remains conscious  

The resulting crystalline mass appears structurally identical to the crystals embedded within the blade.  

**Casualty Record**  

Testing has resulted in extreme losses.  

Confirmed fatalities:  

7,488 AT-Class test subjects  

7 B.E.S.E.R.K. Operators  

All attempts to wield the weapon have ended in immediate subject termination.  

No individual has successfully activated or controlled the artifact.  

**Operational Restrictions**  

Due to the extreme loss rate, the following directive has been issued:  

A.P.A. Operators are strictly prohibited from attempting to wield LD-043.  

The resource cost of training and producing A.P.A. personnel has been deemed too significant to risk further loss.  

Violation of this directive is classified as Level-5 disciplinary breach.  

**Current Research Status**  

The following details remain fully redacted by order of DIVIDE Command:  

Origin of LD-043  

True pulse classification  
 
Potential activation conditions  

Recorded anomalies during early containment attempts  

The true capability of the weapon remains unknown.
