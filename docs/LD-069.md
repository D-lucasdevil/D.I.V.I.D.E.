## Overview  
LD-069, designated **"The Latex Lament,"** is a sapient female humanoid entity manifesting as an extremely disturbing and highly sexualized torturer. The entity is permanently encased in a seamless, pitch-black latex suit that covers her entire body with the exception of her mouth, which appears violently ripped open in a permanent, jagged smile exposing teeth and gums.   
Her eyes are completely sealed beneath the latex, yet she navigates and perceives her surroundings with perfect accuracy. She constantly carries a long, spiked whip made of the same unknown latex-like material, which she has never been observed releasing.     

The entity displays the highest regenerative capability ever recorded by DIVIDE, rendering her effectively immortal. She is self-aware, highly articulate, and claims to be **"the manifestation of your depravity — for love is depravity, isn’t it?"**  

---  

## Discovery  
LD-069 first manifested on **02/06/2026** inside a private underground club in [REDACTED], following multiple simultaneous missing persons reports. Local authorities discovered a massacre site where 47 individuals had been subjected to prolonged, sadistic sexual torture. The entity was found sitting calmly in the center of the carnage, covered in blood and fluids, casually conversing with a half-skinned victim who was still alive.   

DIVIDE rapid response teams engaged immediately. Despite sustaining catastrophic damage (including dismemberment, immolation, and acid immersion), LD-069 regenerated within seconds and expressed gratitude before counterattacking.   

---   

## Initial Containment   
Standard containment protocols failed catastrophically. After 19 failed attempts resulting in 312 personnel casualties, LD-069 was finally restrained by dropping a 40-meter diameter reinforced titanium sphere (Sphere Protocol-09) over her location. The sphere was then buried 300 meters underground in a dedicated blacksite.   

The entity has not attempted escape as long as she is provided with a consistent supply of "guests." The longer she is left without stimulation, the more agitated and creative she becomes in her demands.

## Anomalous Properties   
### Extreme Regeneration   
LD-069 possesses the most advanced regenerative ability on record. She has survived:  
- Complete disintegration by plasma  
- Boiling in superheated liquid for 72 hours  
- Skinning, decapitation, and evisceration  
- Prolonged crushing under hydraulic pressure  
- Exposure to absolute zero conditions    

After every instance of destruction, she fully regenerates within 4–40 seconds, usually while moaning or laughing. She frequently thanks her tormentors with the phrase:     
**"Thank you for this sensation and inspiration."**     
followed by visible orgasmic response.    

### Sexual Sadism & Adaptive Biology   
Upon encountering a target:   
- **Male victims**: She immediately closes distance with unnatural speed, restrains them, and engages in forceful sexual intercourse while simultaneously flaying them alive with her whip or nails. She can amplify the victim’s sensory input to extreme levels, making even light touch feel like agony or unbearable ecstasy.    
- **Female victims**: She manifests a fully functional penis (morphing from her own body) and proceeds with violent penetration while using bladed implements to scrape internal tissue, all while skinning the victim. Sensory amplification is applied identically.    

She displays no fatigue whatsoever. Even after months of continuous combat and dismemberment, she remains at full capacity. Her strength is roughly equivalent to a mid-tier BERSERK operative, but her infinite stamina and regeneration make her overwhelmingly dangerous in prolonged engagements.    

The entity shows no victim discrimination. She has been documented torturing men, women, children, and animals with equal enthusiasm, often stating that "all flesh sings the same beautiful song when opened."

## Behavior & Psychology    
Despite her extreme depravity, LD-069 is highly intelligent, eloquent, and philosophical. She willingly engages in conversation and appears to genuinely enjoy dialogue — until the three-minute mark, after which her behavior shifts into predatory arousal and she begins planning how to "make the conversation more intimate."     

She has demonstrated full consent to any form of torture performed on her. In one recorded incident, when a convicted cannibal (Tasked Asset-774) asked if he could eat her, she immediately ripped open her own abdominal cavity with her hands and invited him to begin. When the asset became horrified and vomited, her demeanor changed instantly. The resulting punishment is now classified as one of the most disturbing torture sessions ever documented.     

She frequently repeats variations of:   
**"I am the manifestation of your depravity. For love is depravity, isn’t it?"**

## Containment Usage   
Following multiple reviews, LD-069 has been authorized for use as a **punishment tool** and psychological deterrent against DIVIDE personnel who commit severe breaches of conduct or attempt defection.   

Subjects sentenced to "Latex Communion" are delivered to the sphere. Duration of exposure is calibrated based on offense severity. No subject has ever returned psychologically intact. Many beg for termination after the first hour.    

---

## Threat Assessment   
LD-069 is classified **Class IV** due to:  
- Effectively immortal regenerative capabilities  
- Extreme sadistic efficiency   
- Psychological contamination risk (survivors often develop self-harm fetishes or total mental collapse)   
- Indiscriminate victim selection   

While not possessing world-ending destructive power like some Class IVs, her ability to break both body and mind makes her one of the most disturbing anomalies in current containment.   

**Note:** Under no circumstances should personnel engage LD-069 in conversation longer than 180 seconds without Level 5 authorization.
