**Threat Level: Class III (Variable — Emotion-Dependent-Doesn't go above class III)**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 4–7 (Based on interaction type)**  
**Containment Status: Restrained / Heavily Monitored**

**Overview:**  

LD-047, designated “Fragment of One’s Imagination” and informally referred to as “Rhythm,” is a humanoid entity exhibiting behavior directly influenced by the emotional state and intent of nearby individuals.  

The entity is considered a dual-state anomaly, capable of both cooperative and lethal outcomes depending entirely on interaction conditions.  

**Physical Description:**  

LD-047 appears as a humanoid female with the following characteristics:  
 
Gray-toned skin  
Yellow, semi-mechanical eyes  
Yellow headphones (constant)  
Black-and-yellow antennae resembling those of a bee  
Dark gray pants with purple striping (front and back)  
Yellow, black, and white boots  
Fur-like collar or neck covering  

No signs of biological necessity (e.g., breathing, fatigue) have been observed.  

**Behavioral States:**  

LD-047 operates in two primary modes:  

 Positive State (Cooperative)  

**Triggered by:**  

Calm, friendly, or positive emotional approach  

**Behavior:**  

Approaches subject  
Greets with visible friendliness (smile, wave)  
Asks: “What do you want to do?”  

**Upon response:**  

A translucent bubble-like field forms around both the entity and the subject  
After several seconds, the bubble collapses (“pops”)  
Both are returned safely  
LD-047 gently removes the subject from the area  
🔴 Negative State (Hostile / Conditional)  

**Triggered by:**  

Aggression  
Fear  
Hostile intent  
Emotional instability  
Behavior:  

Moves toward subject in an agitated manner  
Issues a single objective or command  

A darker, unstable bubble field forms.  

**Outcome Conditions:**  
 Objective Completed:  
Bubble collapses safely  
LD-047 returns to Positive State  
 Objective Failed / Ignored:   
Bubble collapses  
Subject is reduced to:  
A localized pool of blood  
No recoverable remains  
**Containment Interaction Rules:**  
Emotional state must be monitored prior to entry  
Only trained personnel allowed interaction  
**Subjects must:**  
Remain calm  
Follow instructions immediately  
Avoid unpredictable behavior
