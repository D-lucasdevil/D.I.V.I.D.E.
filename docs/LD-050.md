**File: LD-050 — “The Calamity Empress”**  

**Designation: Armored Humanoid / High-Intensity Combat Anomaly**  
**Threat Level: Class IV — Extreme (Conditional Cooperation)**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 5+**  
**Containment Status: Contained — Cooperative Under Conditions**

**Overview:**  

LD-050, designated “The Calamity Empress,” is a heavily armored humanoid anomaly of unknown origin that entered this reality during one of the largest recorded Pulse events.  

Despite her extreme destructive capability, LD-050 is currently contained through voluntary cooperation, making her one of the most dangerous yet temporarily stable anomalies in DIVIDE custody.  

**Physical Description:**  

LD-050 appears as a tall female humanoid entity (~190–210 cm) fully encased in ornate, organic-metal armor.  

**Notable features:**  

Full-body armor with bone-like and chitinous structure  
Deep red cloak-like fabric extending from the back  
Long tail-like appendage with segmented plating  
Helmet resembling a stylized avian or predatory mask  
Exposed hair (dark brown / crimson-tinted)  

**Additional note:**  

LD-050 has never willingly removed her armor in observed conditions  
All attempts to record unarmored state result in:  
Immediate equipment failure  
Data corruption  
Primary Abilities:  
1. Extreme Physical Combat Capability  
Strength far beyond enhanced human levels  
Capable of:  
Tearing through armored units  
Withstanding heavy weaponry  
Demonstrated ability to eliminate:  
4 B.E.S.E.R.K operatives  
~22,000 armed personnel during initial incident  
2. Adaptive Combat Growth  

**LD-050 becomes stronger when:**  

Engaged by multiple opponents  
Sustaining damage  
Prolonged combat exposure  

This effect appears to have a scaling limit, though the threshold is currently unknown.  

3. Regenerative Ability  
Rapid healing from:  
Ballistic damage  
Explosive impact  
Regeneration speed increases under combat stress  

**Current assessment:**  

Effective immortality under battlefield conditions  

4. Thermal Cataclysm (Restricted Ability)  

LD-050 possesses the ability to emit an extreme heat-based breath attack capable of:  

Instantaneous vaporization of large areas  
Confirmed destruction of an entire city  

**Limitations:**  

Causes extreme internal overheating  
Used only as a last resort  
Significant cooldown required  
Behavior & Psychology:  
Highly aggressive under combat conditions  
Displays dominance-based perception of others  
Critical Behavioral Traits:  
Refuses presence of:  
Other females (interpreted as rivals)  
APA-01 (designated hostile target)  
Exhibits selective cooperation:  
Agreed to containment in exchange for:  
Isolation  
Absence of perceived rivals  
Access to leisure items (notably: ice cream)  
Containment Protocol:  
Housed in a remote, isolated facility zone  
Zero female personnel allowed within proximity  
Continuous monitoring via hardened systems  

**Emergency protocol:**  

**In case of breach:**  
APA-01 is to be deployed immediately  
Civilian casualties deemed acceptable to prevent escalation  
Incident Record — Initial Manifestation:  
Arrival triggered major Pulse event  
Immediate large-scale conflict initiated  

**Casualties:**  

4 B.E.S.E.R.K operatives  
~22,000 military personnel  
Entire urban zone destroyed via thermal attack  

Engagement ended only after intervention by:  

APA-01  

**Outcome:**  

LD-050 entered conditional cooperation  
**Threat Assessment:**  

Classified as Class IV due to:  

City-level destruction capability  
Adaptive scaling strength  
Near-immunity to conventional termination  
Psychological instability under provocation  

**Upgrade to higher classification possible under:**  

Sustained combat escalation  
Full release of thermal capability  

**Internal Memorandum:**  

“We didn’t contain her.  
She decided we weren’t worth killing… yet.”  

— DIVIDE High Command
