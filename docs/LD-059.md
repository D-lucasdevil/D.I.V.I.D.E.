# File: LD-059 — “The Murder Bucket”  

**Designation:** Cursed/Inanimate Contact Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** Class II  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 2+  
**Containment Status:** Stable Containment Active  

---  

## Overview  

LD-067, designated **“The Murder Bucket,”** is an anomalous stainless-steel bucket measuring approximately:  

* 26 centimeters in diameter  
* 16 centimeters in height  

The object possesses standard wooden carrying handles and displays no unusual physical characteristics during inactive states.  

Despite its mundane appearance, LD-059 exhibits several lethal anomalous properties involving:    

* Sudden kinetic movement  
* Fatal contact reactions  
* Mild psychological influence  

The anomaly frequently produces abrupt hopping or “jumping” motions resembling popcorn movement inside a heated container.  

These movements occur without warning and may generate enough force to cause serious injury.  

---  

## Primary Anomalous Properties    

### Distressing Aura  

Individuals within close proximity to LD-059 frequently report:  

* Feelings of unease  
* Mild paranoia  
* Sensations of being observed  

Extended exposure may result in:  

* Elevated stress levels  
* Sleep disruption  
* Increased anxiety  

Psychological effects remain relatively minor compared to most hostile anomalies.  

---  

### Sudden Movement  

LD-059 periodically launches itself in random directions at high speed.  

Observed movement includes:  

* Vertical jumps  
* Rapid sliding  
* Rotational impacts  

Injuries caused by impact include:  

* Broken bones  
* Head trauma  
* Severe bruising  

Fatalities remain uncommon unless direct contact occurs simultaneously.  

---  

### Photographic Marker Effect  

Whenever LD-059 appears in photographs or digital recordings, anomalous visual indicators manifest automatically.  

These indicators include:  

* Bright red circles  
* Directional arrows pointing toward the bucket  

The markings appear regardless of:  

* Editing software  
* Camera type  
* Recording format  

This property has proven useful during containment breaches.  

---  

### Fatal Contact Response  

Direct skin contact with LD-067 triggers immediate lethal effects in most organic lifeforms.  

Documented outcomes include:  

* Sudden cardiac arrest  
* Severe internal trauma  
* Catastrophic biological rupture in rare cases  

The effect activates only through confirmed direct physical contact.  

Notably unaffected organisms include:  

* Domestic cats  
* Certain other anomalies  

The reason for feline immunity remains unknown.  

---  

## Safe Handling Procedures  

Testing confirms LD-059 may be moved safely using:  

* Metal tongs  
* Ropes or hooks   
* Remote handling equipment  
* Rubber gloves coated with cat hair  

Direct physical handling remains strictly prohibited.  

---   

## Unsafe Actions  

Personnel are forbidden from:  

* Touching LD-059 directly  
* Holding its handles  
* Kicking or striking the object  
* Attempting manual transportation  

Multiple accidental fatalities have resulted from disregard of these procedures.  

---  

## Containment  

LD-059 is contained within:  

* A reinforced empty storage chamber  

Containment requirements:  

* Minimal furnishing inside containment room  
* Locked access door at all times  
* Remote camera observation only  
* Weekly positional verification  

A small quantity of cat hair is maintained inside containment storage as a precautionary handling measure.  

---  

## Incident Record — Discovery  

LD-059 was initially discovered through undercover DIVIDE surveillance of illegal anomalous trafficking operations known informally as:  

## “The Black Streets”  

An advertisement referencing:  

> “The Bucket Assassin”  

led operatives to the anomaly’s recovery.  

---  

## The Bucket Assassin  

“The Bucket Assassin” was an unidentified 42-year-old contract killer who utilized LD-067 alongside a homemade launching device referred to as:  

## “The Bucket Slinger”  

The device functioned by propelling LD-059 toward intended targets while avoiding direct physical contact.  

The individual reportedly accepted assassination contracts through underground anomalous markets.  

---  

## Recovery Operation  

DIVIDE agents arranged a fabricated contract in order to apprehend the suspect and secure LD-059.  

During the operation:  

* The suspect became startled upon interception  
* LD-059 was accidentally dropped onto his foot while loading the launcher  

The resulting fatality was immediate and extreme.  

Due to the condition of the remains, investigators were unable to recover:  

* Legal identity  
* Residence information  
* Family records    

The only known personal detail recovered was repeated complaints regarding a prior divorce during negotiations with undercover personnel.  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-059 is classified as:  

## **Class II**  

Reasoning:  

* Highly lethal upon direct contact  
* Dangerous but localized effects  
* Limited mobility  
* Easily containable under proper procedures  

While individually deadly, LD-059 lacks:  

* Intelligence  
* Large-scale destructive capability  
* Escalation potential  

The anomaly primarily represents a containment hazard rather than an extinction-level threat.
