# File: LD-081 — "The Corrosive Drake"   

**Designation:** Draconic Reptilian Anomaly   

**Threat Level:** Class III   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+   

**Containment Status:** Contained — Acid-Resistant Habitat   

---   

## Overview   

LD-081, designated **"The Corrosive Drake,"** is a highly intelligent draconic anomaly resembling a juvenile dragon measuring approximately **3.6 meters** from snout to tail with a wingspan of **6.2 meters**. Despite its relatively small size compared to mythological dragons, LD-081 is an extremely dangerous predator due to its exceptional mobility, corrosive biological weaponry, and remarkable adaptability.   

Unlike traditional depictions of dragons, LD-081 is incapable of producing fire. Instead, specialized organs within its throat synthesize an unknown acidic compound capable of dissolving nearly every naturally occurring material currently tested by DIVIDE.   

---   

## Physical Description    

The creature possesses a lean, predatory frame covered in overlapping black and charcoal-colored scales with pale ivory plating protecting its head and upper spine.    

Its most notable characteristics include:     

* Four powerful clawed limbs.    
* Long muscular tail used for balance and striking.    
* Two folded wings capable of silent flight.   
* Curved ivory horns.   
* Multiple rows of serrated teeth continuously replaced throughout its lifetime.    
* Glowing yellow eyes with slit pupils capable of seeing in complete darkness.     

Although appearing reptilian, internal scans reveal organs unlike any known terrestrial animal.   

---   
 
## Discovery    
 
LD-081 first appeared during a localized **Class III Pulse Event** deep within the Carpathian mountain range.    

Several livestock disappearances led local authorities to investigate abandoned caves where unusually smooth tunnels and pools of partially dissolved stone were discovered.   

A BESERK retrieval team entered the cave expecting a standard predatory anomaly.    

Within eleven minutes, four operatives suffered severe chemical injuries after the creature collapsed portions of the cave using concentrated acid before retreating into narrow passages inaccessible to humans.    

The anomaly was eventually captured after PHANTOM Squad deployed specialized cryogenic foam, temporarily reducing its body temperature enough to suppress acid production.    

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### Corrosive Breath    

Instead of fire, LD-081 expels a highly pressurized stream of emerald-colored acid.    

Observed effects include:    

* Dissolving reinforced steel in under thirty seconds.    
* Melting concrete.    
* Destroying most biological tissue almost instantly.    
* Remaining chemically active for several minutes after impact.   

The acid loses effectiveness after prolonged exposure to extremely low temperatures.    

---    

### Adaptive Digestion    

LD-081 is capable of consuming:    

* Stone   
* Metal   
* Bone   
* Organic matter    
* Certain anomalous materials    

Researchers believe nearly all consumed matter is broken down into raw energy used to replenish its internal acid reserves.   

---    

### Silent Flight    

Despite its size, LD-081 produces almost no detectable sound while airborne very similar to owls.    

Its wings possess specialized membranes that significantly reduce turbulence, allowing the creature to ambush prey with remarkable efficiency.   

---   

### Intelligence   

LD-081 demonstrates intelligence comparable to that of great apes.    

It has been observed:   

* Setting ambushes.   
* Remembering personnel.    
* Recognizing containment routines.   
* Pretending to sleep before attacking.    

Repeated testing suggests it understands simple spoken commands but deliberately ignores most of them.    

---    

## Behavior    

Unlike many predatory anomalies, LD-081 rarely attacks without reason.   

When left undisturbed it spends most of its time sleeping, climbing containment walls, or collecting unusual objects into organized piles.    

Its favorite objects include:   

* Polished stones.   
* Metallic scraps.   
* Pocket watches.   
* Empty ammunition casings.   

Personnel have nicknamed these collections its "treasure."    

Removing items from the pile almost always provokes aggressive behavior.    

---    

## Containment    

LD-081 is housed inside a reinforced cavern lined entirely with corrosion-resistant ceramic alloys.   

Containment protocols include:    

* Automated feeding systems.    
* Constant temperature regulation.    
* Remote observation only.    
* Emergency cryogenic suppression systems.    

No personnel are permitted to enter containment without PHANTOM escort.    
 
---   

## Threat Assessment   

LD-081 receives a **Class III** designation because:    

* It is capable of killing heavily equipped personnel.    
* Its acid can compromise containment structures.    
* It is highly intelligent and difficult to corner.    

However, unlike higher-tier entities, LD-081 possesses clear physical limits.    

High-caliber weaponry remains effective, and sufficient injuries will incapacitate or terminate the anomaly.    

---    

## Research Notes    

Despite its danger, researchers consider LD-081 one of the more stable predatory anomalies currently contained.   

It has never displayed unnecessary cruelty and appears motivated primarily by hunger, curiosity, and territorial behavior.   

Several researchers have proposed that, if raised from hatching, individuals of the same species might be trainable.   

Proposal remains **Denied**.
