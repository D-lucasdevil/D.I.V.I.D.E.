# File: LD-061 — “The Gravebound Colossus”  

**Designation:** Titan-Class Predatory Humanoid  
**Threat Level:** Class III  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Extreme Regional Monitoring Active

## Overview  

LD-061, designated **“The Gravebound Colossus,”** is a massive humanoid anomaly resembling a heavily decayed fur-covered giant measuring approximately:  

## 11–13 meters in height  

The entity possesses:  

* Dense grey-white fur  
* Abnormally enlarged forelimbs  
* Exposed flesh beneath damaged hide  
* Massive clawed extremities capable of crushing armored vehicles  

Large quantities of:  

* Rusted weapons  
* Bones  
* Shields  
* Human skulls  

have been found embedded directly into the creature’s body.  

Many of these objects appear fused into the flesh itself rather than externally attached.  

The anomaly’s head resembles a partially decomposed bear-like organism, though no known biological species matches its structure.  

LD-061 is most commonly sighted in:  

* Frozen mountain ranges  
* Snow-covered battlefields  
* Isolated northern territories  

The entity leaves behind massive trails of crushed terrain and frozen corpses during migration periods.  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-061 demonstrates territorial and predatory behavior.  

Unlike many humanoid anomalies, it does not hunt for consumption alone.  

The entity appears to deliberately collect:  

* Weapons  
* Armor  
* Corpses  
* Skeletal remains  

These materials are gradually absorbed into its body over time.  

Witnesses report the creature frequently:  

* Remaining motionless during snowstorms  
* Digging through ancient battlegrounds  
* Carrying corpses over long distances  
* Sleeping partially buried beneath ice and stone  

LD-061 becomes highly aggressive when:  

* Approached directly  
* Attacked with artillery  
* Prevented from recovering remains from battle zones  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Extreme Physical Strength  

LD-061 possesses enough force to:  

* Tear armored vehicles apart  
* Collapse defensive structures  
* Crush reinforced concrete with direct impact  

Single strikes have been recorded generating localized seismic activity.  

---  

### Cold Zone Generation  

The anomaly emits extreme cold surrounding its body.  

Observed effects include:   

* Sudden temperature drops  
* Rapid frost formation  
* Frozen organic tissue upon contact  
* Mechanical freezing in nearby equipment  

Blizzards frequently form around the entity during prolonged activity.  

---  

### Bone and Metal Assimilation  

LD-061 can absorb nearby:  

* Bones  
* Weapons  
* Metallic debris  

into its own body mass.  

Assimilated material appears to reinforce:  

* Muscle density  
* Defensive durability  
* Regenerative capability  

Embedded weapons occasionally emerge from the flesh during combat.  

---  

### Regeneration  

The entity demonstrates slow but highly effective regeneration.  

Wounds recover faster when:  

* Surrounded by corpses  
* Located near battlefields  
* Exposed to low temperatures  

Severed fur and flesh have been observed partially reconstructing over several hours.  

---  

## Weakness  

Despite its durability, LD-061 demonstrates vulnerability to:  

* Sustained thermal weaponry  
* High-yield incendiary attacks  
* Prolonged environmental heat exposure  

Extreme heat significantly slows:  

* Regeneration  
* Movement speed  
* Assimilation processes  

Molten industrial compounds proved moderately effective during containment attempts.  

---  

## Incident Record — The White Valley Massacre  

The largest confirmed encounter occurred after multiple villages disappeared within a northern mountain region.  

Recovery teams discovered:  

* Entire forests flattened  
* Hundreds of frozen corpses  
* Massive piles of collected weapons and skeletal remains  

DIVIDE assault forces engaged LD-061 using:  

* Heavy artillery  
* Incendiary bombardment  
* anti-titan weapon systems  

Confirmed losses included:  

* 312 military personnel  
* 17 ECHO-operatives  
* 6 armored transport units  

The entity retreated after sustained thermal bombardment triggered structural collapse across the surrounding mountain ridge.  

---  

## Containment Attempts  

Direct containment has failed repeatedly due to:  

* Immense physical strength  
* Environmental adaptability  
* Long-range migration patterns  

Current DIVIDE strategy focuses on:  

* Satellite tracking  
* Battle-zone denial operations  
* Preventing access to large corpse concentrations  

If LD-061 is to kill too many humans BESERK is to be sent to destroy and kill LD-061 permanently as of now protocol is to study it.  

Civilian evacuation remains the primary response method during manifestations.    

---    

## Threat Assessment    

LD-061 is classified as:    

## **Class III**  

Reasoning:  

* Massive physical destructive capability  
* Environmental hazard generation  
* Extreme combat durability  

However:  

* Limited intelligence compared to higher anomalies  
* Slow movement speed  
* Lack of large-scale reality manipulation
