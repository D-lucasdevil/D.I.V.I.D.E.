# File: LD-056 — “The Hollow Seraph”  

**Designation:** Aerial/Psychological Predatory Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** Class III  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+  
**Containment Status:** Unstable Observation Containment Active

## Overview  

LD-056, designated **“The Hollow Seraph,”** is a humanoid aerial anomaly associated with mass hallucinations, nocturnal disappearances, and localized psychological collapse events.  

The entity resembles a severely emaciated winged humanoid approximately 2.7 meters in height possessing:  

* Oversized black feathered wings  
* Reverse-jointed lower limbs  
* Elongated fingers ending in hooked claws  
* A partially obscured humanoid face hidden beneath long black hair  

The anomaly’s eyes emit faint white luminescence visible even in complete darkness.  


Instead, witnesses report:  

* Sudden hovering without motion  
* Silent aerial drifting  
* Abrupt repositioning between structures  
* Brief disappearances resembling visual distortion rather than movement  

The entity is most commonly encountered near:  

* Abandoned churches  
* Rooftops  
* Forest edges  
* Hospitals during power outages  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-056 demonstrates unusually selective predatory behavior.  

Unlike aggressive hunting anomalies, it appears drawn toward:  

* Emotionally distressed individuals  
* Isolated persons  
* Areas experiencing grief-related events  

Prior to manifestation, affected individuals frequently report:  

* Sleep paralysis  
* Repeated dreams of wings or falling  
* Hearing scratching sounds above ceilings  
* Seeing glowing eyes in reflections  

The anomaly often remains motionless while observing targets for extended periods before engagement.  

During encounters:  

* Direct eye contact significantly worsens psychological symptoms  
* Victims become increasingly detached from reality  
* Hallucinations intensify over time  

Recovered survivors describe overwhelming sensations of:  

* Dread  
* Hopelessness  
* “Being watched from above”  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Psychological Destabilization  

LD-056 emits an unidentified cognitive influence affecting nearby individuals.  

Observed symptoms include:  

* Auditory hallucinations  
* Severe paranoia  
* Emotional suppression  
* Visual distortions involving shadows or feathers  

Extended exposure may result in:  

* Catatonic behavior  
* Self-isolation  
* Complete mental collapse  

---  

### Silent Descent  

The anomaly can descend from elevated positions without generating:  

* Sound  
* Wind displacement  
* Detectable thermal fluctuation  

Security footage often fails to capture full movement between frames.  

Witnesses typically perceive LD-056 only moments before physical contact occurs.  

---  

### Fear Feeding  

Research suggests LD-056 becomes physically stronger through prolonged psychological exposure rather than direct physical violence but it still has to eat.  

Signs of escalation include:  

* Wing expansion  
* Increased eye luminosity  
* Faster movement speed  
* More aggressive hallucination effects  

Unlike most predatory anomalies, LD-056 prefers targets to remain alive during this process.  

---  

### Shadow Distortion  

Under low-light conditions, LD-056 can partially blend into surrounding darkness.  

Observed effects:  

* Inconsistent visual outline  
* Apparent body distortion  
* Temporary invisibility in poorly lit environments  

Infrared imaging remains partially effective.  

---  

## Weakness  

LD-056 demonstrates reduced activity under:  

* High-intensity lighting  
* Continuous human observation ussualy chosing to kill instead of mentaly breaking multiple people but it will try  
* Large crowds  

The anomaly appears unable to maintain stable manifestation when exposed to sustained direct illumination.  

Floodlight deployments during containment operations significantly impaired:  

* Mobility  
* Hallucination effects  
* Shadow distortion abilities  

Research also suggests the entity avoids heavily populated environments.  

---

## Incident Record — “The Black Choir Event”  

The largest confirmed manifestation occurred following a citywide power failure affecting multiple residential sectors.  

Emergency reports described:  

* Winged silhouettes on rooftops  
* Residents refusing to leave darkened homes  
* Multiple simultaneous psychological breakdowns  

Confirmed casualties:  

* 19 civilian deaths  
* 43 severe psychological collapse cases  
* 3 DIVIDE operatives incapacitated during pursuit  

Recovered security recordings repeatedly displayed:  

* Single-frame appearances of glowing eyes  
* Distorted wing-like shadows  
* Static corruption immediately before disappearances  

---  

## Containment Operation  

DIVIDE containment teams successfully forced retreat conditions using:  

* Industrial floodlight arrays  
* Mobile thermal surveillance systems  
* Continuous auditory disruption broadcasts  

During the operation:  

* LD-056 demonstrated extreme agitation under direct illumination  
* Wing structures appeared partially deteriorative  
* The entity retreated into upper urban infrastructure zones before disappearing entirely  

Permanent containment has not yet been achieved.  

---

## Containment Protocols  

Current containment procedures include:  

* 24-hour rooftop surveillance in active zones  
* Emergency floodlight deployment systems  
* Mandatory psychological evaluations for exposed personnel  
* Sleep monitoring for nearby civilian populations  

Emergency protocol: 

If multiple individuals report:  

* Winged hallucinations  
* Ceiling scratching noises  
* Shared visual manifestations  

Then:  

* Area blackout restrictions initiated    
* Echo-9 rapid response deployment authorized  
* Civilian evacuation recommended  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-056 is classified as:  

## **Class III**  

Reasoning:  

* Dangerous psychological influence  
* Effective stealth and ambush capabilities  
* Moderate casualty potential  
* Strong fear-based escalation behavior  

However:  

* Limited direct destructive capability  
* Vulnerable to intense illumination  
* Reduced effectiveness in populated environments  

Under prepared operational conditions, elite DIVIDE units can suppress manifestations successfully.
