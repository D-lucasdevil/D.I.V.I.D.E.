# File: LD-057 — “Egg of Concepts”  

**Designation:** Conceptual/Reality-Reactive Anomalous Object  
**Threat Level:** Class II  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 3+  
**Containment Status:** Stable Containment Active — Cross-Facility Restriction Enforced  

---  

## Overview  

LD-057, designated **“Egg of Concepts,”** is an anomalous oval-shaped object composed of unidentified shifting matter displaying constantly rotating layers of color and light.  

The object resembles a large egg approximately 1.2 meters in height suspended slightly above nearby surfaces through unknown means.  

Surface analysis has failed to identify:  

* Stable molecular structure  
* Internal biological systems  
* Consistent physical composition  

Despite this, LD-057 displays no hostile behavior under standard conditions.  

Personnel stationed near the anomaly for prolonged periods report mild beneficial effects including:  

* Improved morale  
* Reduced fatigue  
* Increased emotional stability  
* Lower aggression levels among contained anomalies  

Multiple facilities requested relocation of LD-057 after measurable decreases in containment incidents were observed.  

Research suggests the anomaly emits a low-level conceptual stabilization effect influencing nearby environments.  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-057 remains inactive unless physically interacted with.  

Upon contact:  

* Surface colors begin accelerating rapidly  
* Internal light distortion intensifies  
* A random manifestation event occurs shortly afterward  

Manifestations vary significantly in severity and structure.  

Documented outcomes include:  

### Minor Positive Events  

* Sudden appearance of food or tools  
* Temporary equipment self-repair  
* Increased physical stamina  
* Psychological calmness  

### Neutral Events  

* Appearance of harmless objects  
* Localized gravity fluctuations  
* Temporary color distortion in nearby rooms  

### Negative Events  

* Electrical failures  
* Short-lived hostile entities  
* Spatial instability lasting several seconds  
* Random anomalous material generation  

Results appear partially influenced by:  

* Emotional state of the interactee  
* Environmental instability  
* Unknown probability variables  

No manifestation has exceeded localized containment capacity under normal conditions.  

---  

## Primary Properties  

### Conceptual Reaction  

LD-057 does not appear to “create” objects conventionally.  

Instead, research indicates it temporarily manifests:  

* Possibilities  
* Concepts  
* Hypothetical outcomes  

Most generated phenomena collapse naturally within minutes or hours.  

---  

### Stabilization Field  

A passive field surrounding LD-057 reduces:  

* Aggression in nearby anomalies  
* Emotional instability in personnel  
* Minor reality distortions  

This effect weakens significantly beyond 40 meters.  

---  

### Probability Distortion  

Repeated interaction with LD-057 slightly increases the likelihood of improbable events occurring nearby.  

Observed examples include:  

* Equipment functioning despite severe damage  
* Personnel surviving otherwise fatal accidents  
* Random containment system malfunctions  

For this reason, unnecessary interaction is prohibited.  

---  

## Special Restriction — LD-047 Interaction  

Under no circumstances is LD-057 permitted within the same operational zone as:  

* LD-047 — “Fragment of One’s Imagination”  

Research predicts conceptual resonance between the two anomalies could trigger catastrophic amplification effects.  

Simulations suggest LD-047 could potentially use LD-057 to stabilize and expand her existing abilities beyond current containment projections.  

Potential effects include:  

* Large-scale spatial distortion  
* Pocket-dimension stabilization  
* Extreme mobility enhancement  
* Advanced conceptual manipulation  

Threat projection in such an event escalates to:  

## **Class IV Hazard Potential**  

Probability of successful containment under those conditions remains extremely low.  

---  

## Protocol Butterfly  

Protocol Butterfly is a last-resort emergency response procedure authorized only if:  

* LD-047 gains physical contact with LD-057  

Protocol details remain:  

## **[DATA EXPUNGED]**  

Known objectives include:  

* Immediate anomaly separation  
* Facility-wide lockdown  
* Conceptual suppression deployment  
* Authorization of extreme containment measures  

No live testing has been approved.  

---  

## Incident Record — Probability Cascade Event  

During unauthorized testing, a researcher activated LD-057 twelve consecutive times.  

Resulting effects included:  

* Temporary gravity inversion  
* Simultaneous power restoration and failure across multiple sectors  
* Manifestation of unidentified hostile organisms  
* Sudden spontaneous growth of vegetation inside containment halls  

One researcher reportedly vanished for approximately 11 seconds before reappearing unharmed.  

The individual later claimed:  

> “I saw rooms that do not exist yet.”  

Psychological monitoring remains ongoing.  

---  

## Containment Protocols  

LD-057 is stored within:  

* A low-interference conceptual containment chamber  

Containment requirements:  

* Maximum two authorized interactions per week  
* Emotional instability screenings mandatory before testing  
* No overlapping containment routes with LD-047 facilities  
* Automated observation systems active continuously  

Emergency protocol:  

If conceptual instability exceeds acceptable thresholds:  

* Chamber isolation initiated  
* Interaction privileges suspended  
* conceptual hazard teams deployed  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-057 is classified as:  

## **Class II**  

Reasoning:  

* Non-hostile baseline behavior  
* Beneficial passive effects  
* Limited direct destructive capability  
* Controlled manifestations remain manageable  

However:  

* Interaction unpredictability remains dangerous  
* Probability distortion poses containment risks  
* Combination with LD-047 creates severe escalation potential  

The anomaly itself is considered low-threat.  

Its greatest danger lies in:  

## **What it could enable.**
