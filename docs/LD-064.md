# File: LD-064 — “The Veiled Queen & The Devourer”  

**Designation:** Symbiotic Dual-Entity Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** Class IV  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Separation Attempts Ongoing  

---  

## Overview  

LD-064 is a dual-entity anomaly consisting of two separate organisms designated:  

* **LD-064-A — "The Veiled Queen"**  
* **LD-064-B — "The Devourer"**  

While technically independent entities, both organisms exhibit a permanent anomalous bond and have never been observed more than 50 meters apart.  

Attempts to separate the pair have consistently resulted in catastrophic containment failures.  

Researchers now classify them as a single anomaly system.  

---  

# LD-064-A — "The Veiled Queen"  

LD-064-A appears as a humanoid female approximately:  

* 2.3 meters tall  
* Pale skin  
* White hair  
* Black organic armor-like growths  
* A floating crimson halo composed of unknown energy  

The entity displays human-level intelligence and is capable of speech in multiple languages.  


The Queen demonstrates remarkable calmness even during combat situations.  

---  

# LD-064-B — "The Devourer"  

LD-064-B is a massive predatory organism measuring:  

* Approximately 8 meters tall  
* Unknown total mass  
* Extreme muscular density  

The creature possesses:  

* Multiple jaws  
* Compound skeletal growths  
* Numerous claws and appendages  
* Continuous biological mutation  

Unlike LD-064-A, the Devourer possesses little observable intelligence.  

Its primary behavior consists of:  

* Hunting  
* Feeding  
* Defending LD-064-A  

Researchers frequently describe the entity as:  

> "A living siege weapon."  

---  

## Symbiotic Link  

The most dangerous aspect of LD-064 is the connection between both entities.  

Injury to one immediately affects the other.  

Examples include:  

* Damage to LD-064-A increasing aggression in LD-064-B  
* Damage to LD-064-B causing severe emotional distress in LD-064-A  
* Separation causing physical deterioration in both organisms  

If the distance between the pair exceeds:  

## 50 meters  

both entities enter a violent state designated:  

### Bond Collapse Event  

During this state:  

* LD-064-B becomes uncontrollably aggressive  
* LD-064-A begins emitting destructive energy surges  
* Nearby electronics fail  
* Casualties increase dramatically  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Crimson Halo  

The halo surrounding LD-064-A generates an unknown form of energy.  

Observed capabilities include:  

* Defensive barriers  
* Projectile interception  
* Limited levitation  
* Energy blasts  

The halo appears directly linked to the Queen's emotional state.  

---  

### Biological Evolution  

LD-064-B continuously adapts to threats.  

Documented adaptations include:  

* Armor plating  
* Enhanced regeneration  
* Increased limb density  
* Resistance to previously effective weaponry  

Adaptations may occur within minutes.  

---  

### Shared Regeneration  

Both entities possess regenerative capabilities.  

If one organism remains alive, recovery rates increase dramatically.  

Researchers believe permanently eliminating one without eliminating the other may be impossible.  

---  

### Guardian Response  

Whenever LD-064-A is threatened:  

LD-064-B enters an extreme combat state.  

Observed effects include:  

* Massive strength increases  
* Enhanced speed  
* Total disregard for injury  

This response continues until the threat is neutralized.  

---  

## Weaknesses  

While powerful, LD-064 possesses several limitations.  

### Dependence  

The pair rely heavily on one another.  

Forced separation weakens both entities.  

Unfortunately, prolonged separation also produces Bond Collapse Events.  

---  

## Incident Record — Operation Broken Crown  

The largest recorded encounter occurred after LD-064 appeared near an abandoned military installation.  

Containment forces attempted to isolate LD-064-A using aerial deployment barriers.  

Initial operation appeared successful.  

Three minutes later:  

* LD-064-B breached four reinforced containment walls  
* Thirty-six BESERK personnel were killed  
* 11 876 millitary personel suffered total casualties  

When the separation reached approximately 120 meters:  

A Bond Collapse Event occurred.  

The resulting energy discharge destroyed most of the containment site.  

Both entities escaped.  

---  

## Containment Protocols  

Current containment doctrine prohibits forced separation.  

Authorized procedures include:  

* Long-range observation  
* Satellite tracking  
* Civilian evacuation  
* Non-engagement unless absolutely necessary  

If direct engagement becomes unavoidable:  

* Simultaneous suppression of both entities is mandatory  
* Separation attempts are forbidden without Director approval  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-064 is classified as:  

# **Class IV**  

Reasoning:  

* Extreme combat capability  
* Near-impossible containment  
* Rapid adaptation  
* Symbiotic regeneration  
* Severe consequences if separated  

However:  

* The anomaly remains localized  
* Neither entity has displayed extinction-level abilities  
* Aggression is generally reactive rather than proactive  

Researchers believe the greatest danger is not either entity individually.  

It is the bond between them.
