# File: LD-063 — “The Hollow Stag”  

**Designation:** Predatory Mimic Entity  
**Threat Level:** Class III  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Active Tracking Ongoing  


![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HJjSJCdXsAA1cL9?format=jpg&name=large)



---  

## Overview  

LD-063, designated **“The Hollow Stag,”** is a highly aggressive predatory anomaly most commonly encountered within dense forests, mountain woodlands, and remote wilderness regions.  

The entity stands approximately:  

* 2.7–3.4 meters tall  
* Digitigrade stance  
* Estimated mass: 300–450 kg  

LD-063 possesses a heavily deformed cervid-like skull fused directly to its head. Examination of recovered biological samples suggests the skull is not a mask but a natural component of the organism's anatomy.  

Notable features include:  

* Elongated jaws lined with multiple rows of needle-like teeth  
* Large forward-facing eyes  
* Black fur covering most of the body  
* Oversized claws capable of penetrating steel sheeting  
* Antler structures displaying constant growth  

The anomaly is almost exclusively nocturnal.  

---  

## Behavior  

Unlike many predatory anomalies, LD-063 demonstrates remarkable patience.  

The entity has been observed: 

* Watching campsites for several days  
* Following hikers without attacking  
* Remaining motionless for extended periods  

Researchers believe LD-063 studies potential prey before engaging. 

The anomaly appears to prefer isolated targets.  

Groups larger than six individuals are rarely attacked.  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Mimicked Voices  

LD-063 can perfectly replicate sounds it has previously heard.  

Documented examples include:  

* Human speech  
* Crying children  
* Emergency radio broadcasts  
* Animal distress calls  

Victims frequently report hearing:  

> "Help me." 

> "I'm over here."  

> "Please don't leave me."  

The voices often originate from impossible locations deep within forests.  

---  

### Extreme Ambush Speed  

Despite its size, LD-063 is capable of explosive bursts of speed.  

Recorded movement speeds exceed:  

## 70 km/h    

during short-distance charges.  

The anomaly typically attacks from concealment before victims become aware of its presence.   

---  

### Bone Consumption   

After feeding, LD-063 consumes:   

* Flesh  
* Bones  
* Clothing  
* Personal equipment  

Very little biological evidence remains following a successful hunt.  

This behavior has complicated population estimates.  

---  

### Antler Growth  

The entity's antlers continuously grow throughout its life.  

Recovered antler fragments possess extreme durability and are comparable to hardened steel.  

Several specimens recovered from old hunting grounds suggest some individuals may survive for centuries.  

---  

## Weaknesses  

LD-063 demonstrates several exploitable limitations:  

* Bright floodlights cause temporary disorientation  
* Fire is actively avoided  
* The anomaly refuses to enter heavily populated urban areas  

Researchers believe LD-063 relies heavily on darkness and concealment for successful predation.  

---  

## Incident Record — Black Pine National Forest  

The largest documented encounter occurred after thirty-two disappearances across a six-month period.   

Investigation teams discovered:  

* Abandoned campsites  
* Disturbed tree lines  
* Unusual antler fragments   

A surveillance drone later captured footage of LD-063 carrying an adult elk while moving on two legs.  

When confronted by personnel, the anomaly killed three operatives before retreating into mountainous terrain.  

The body was never recovered.  

---  

## Containment Efforts  

Direct containment attempts have failed due to:  

* Remote habitat selection  
* Exceptional mobility  
* Low encounter frequency  

Current strategy focuses on:  

* Satellite monitoring  
* Thermal surveillance  
* Civilian evacuation procedures  

Several wilderness regions have been quietly closed following confirmed sightings.  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-063 is classified as:  

## **Class III**  

Reasoning:

* Highly lethal to civilians  
* Difficult to locate  
* Exceptional ambush predator  
* Significant regional threat  

However:  

* Limited intelligence  
* No large-scale destructive abilities  
* Avoidance of populated areas  

The anomaly is considered a severe wilderness hazard rather than a global threat.
