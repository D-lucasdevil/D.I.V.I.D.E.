# File: LD-068 — “The Absolute Zero Blade”  

**Designation:** Anomalous Weapon (Katana)    
**Threat Level:** Class IV   
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 7+   
**Containment Status:** Lost — Active Retrieval Operation    




![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HJwI5OLWcAAlpkn?format=jpg&name=medium)



---    
 
## Overview  

LD-068, designated **"The Absolute Zero Blade,"** is an anomalous katana of unknown origin capable of generating temperatures approaching absolute zero.  

The weapon resembles a traditional Japanese katana measuring approximately:  

* 104 cm in total length  
* 72 cm blade length  

The blade itself appears composed of an unknown silver-white metal permanently coated in frost.  

Despite extensive analysis, no known material matches its composition.  

---  

## Discovery  

LD-068 first appeared on:  

## 01/06/2026  

Following a confirmed Class IV Pulse Event.  

The pulse devastated approximately 14 square kilometers of forest, instantly freezing vegetation, wildlife, and surrounding terrain.  

Upon arrival, DIVIDE personnel discovered the katana embedded within the center of the impact zone.  

No signs of its origin were found.  

---  

## Initial Containment  

LD-068 was transported to a specialized containment facility.  

Within hours of containment, researchers documented a growing thermal anomaly.  

Temperatures continuously decreased regardless of environmental conditions.  

After thirty-seven days:  

* The containment zone reached temperatures beyond measurable standards.  
* Electronic sensors ceased functioning.  
* Multiple thermal systems failed.  

The frozen region eventually expanded to approximately:  

## 1 Kilometer Radius  

No personnel were capable of approaching the containment chamber without suffering immediate catastrophic frost injuries.  

As a result, direct study of LD-068 was largely abandoned.  

---  

## Anomalous Properties  

LD-068 continuously emits extreme cold.  

Unlike conventional temperature reduction, the anomaly appears to directly remove thermal energy from its surroundings.  

Researchers have theorized the blade may partially violate known thermodynamic laws.  

Current estimates suggest temperatures near the blade approach:  

## −273.15°C  

Although accurate measurement remains impossible.  

---  

## Wielder Rejection  

One of the most unusual properties of LD-068 is its apparent refusal to be wielded.  

Prior to the containment breach event, a total of:  

## 17,844  

attempts were made to interact with the weapon.  

Every attempt resulted in immediate death.  

Observed outcomes included:  

* Total body freezing  
* Cellular crystallization  
* Instant organ failure  
* Structural disintegration  

No individual survived contact.  

---

# Incident 068-A  

Four hours following containment, a coordinated terrorist assault targeted the facility.  

Security footage revealed a single unidentified female infiltrator.  

The individual:  

* Bypassed multiple security systems    
* Eliminated numerous armed personnel  
* Reached the frozen containment sector alone  

No existing database has successfully identified the subject.  

---  

## The Acceptance Event  

Surveillance footage recorded the individual walking directly into the frozen exclusion zone.  

Medical analysis of recovered recordings revealed:  

* Complete frostbite  
* Extensive tissue destruction  
* Severe skeletal damage  
* Loss of the left leg during approach  

Despite these injuries, the subject continued advancing.  

Upon reaching LD-068:  

The blade's behavior changed for the first time in recorded history.  

The weapon did not kill the subject.  

Instead, an event now designated:  

## Acceptance  

occurred.  

Recorded effects included:  

* Instant regeneration  
* Hair pigmentation turning white  
* Skin becoming pale  
* Eye coloration changing to crimson  
* Complete recovery of all injuries  

Simultaneously, the frozen zone expanded from:  

## 1 Kilometer  

to  

## 5 Kilometers  

within seconds.  

---  

## Current Host  

The unidentified female is now designated:  

### LD-068-A  

Researchers believe the weapon and its wielder have formed a permanent bond.  

LD-068-A demonstrates:  

* Superhuman speed  
* Extreme cold resistance  
* Accelerated regeneration  
* Complete immunity to temperatures generated by LD-068  

The subject appears physically human despite these alterations.  

---  

## Behavior  

Despite possessing immense destructive capability, LD-068-A has displayed surprisingly selective aggression.  

Documented encounters show:  

* Avoidance of civilian casualties  
* No attacks on non-hostile personnel  
* Preference for evasion over combat  

Several engagements with BERSERK units ended without fatalities directly caused by the subject.  

Most casualties resulted from environmental freezing rather than intentional attacks.  

---  

## Anomaly Hunting Activity  

Since acquiring LD-068, the subject has repeatedly targeted hostile anomalies.  

A pattern has emerged suggesting deliberate selection.  

Researchers remain uncertain whether:  

* LD-068-A chooses the targets  
* LD-068 chooses the targets  
* Both are acting together  

As of this writing, no high-priority containment assets have been attacked.  

---  

## Containment Attempts  

Multiple retrieval operations have failed.  

Including:  

* Three BERSERK deployments  
* Numerous autonomous systems  

No operation has successfully recovered LD-068.  

The frozen environment surrounding the anomaly continues to neutralize most containment methods.  

---  

## Emergency Directive  

If LD-068-A attempts to target critical DIVIDE assets, the following protocol becomes active:  

### Protocol Winter's End  

Authorized response:  

* Immediate APA-01 deployment  
* Full APA task force mobilization  
* Maximum anomaly suppression authorization  

This protocol has never been enacted.  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-068 is classified as:  

# Class IV  

Reasoning:  

* Massive environmental destruction potential  
* Near-impossible containment  
* Unknown upper temperature limits  
* Host enhancement effects  

However:  

* Activity remains localized  
* Subject demonstrates restraint  
* No extinction-level behavior observed  

Further escalation may require reclassification.

# Additional Designation  

### LD-068-A — "The Winter Maiden"  

Following Incident 068-A, the unidentified female who successfully bonded with LD-068 received her own designation.  

All known identification efforts have failed.  

No records of her existence have been discovered in:  

* Government databases  
* Civilian records  
* Military archives  
* DIVIDE systems  
* Anomalous registries  

Researchers remain uncertain whether LD-068-A was ever fully human.  

---  

## Acceptance Event  

Security footage confirms LD-068-A traversed the entire frozen exclusion zone surrounding LD-068 without protective equipment.  

Analysis revealed:  

* Complete frostbite of all limbs  
* Multiple organ failures  
* Extensive skeletal fractures  
* Total destruction of the left leg below the knee  

Despite these injuries, the subject continued advancing toward the weapon.  

Personnel expected death within seconds.  

Instead, upon making contact with LD-068, the anomaly accepted her.  

The moment contact occurred:  

* The subject's severed leg regenerated.  
* All injuries disappeared.  
* Hair pigmentation became completely white.  
* Skin became unnaturally pale.  
* Eye coloration shifted to crimson red.  

Simultaneously the frozen zone expanded from:  

**1 kilometer**  
to  
**5 kilometers**  

within moments.  

This remains the single largest thermal escalation event associated with LD-068.  

---  

## Current Behavior  

Since obtaining LD-068, the subject has repeatedly appeared near anomalous hotspots throughout the world.  

The subject appears to actively hunt specific anomalies.  

Selection criteria remain unknown.  

Notably:  

* Numerous hostile anomalies have disappeared following her appearance.  
* Several Class III anomalies were later found frozen solid.  
* Multiple containment breaches ended shortly after sightings of LD-068-A.  

No evidence suggests the subject is acting on behalf of DIVIDE.  

Equally, no evidence suggests she is actively hostile toward humanity.
