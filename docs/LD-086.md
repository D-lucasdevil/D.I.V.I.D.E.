# File: LD-086 — "The Hook Embrace"   

**Designation:** Anomalous Predator   

**Threat Level:** Class II   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+    

**Containment Status:** Stable    

---   

## Overview    

LD-086, designated **"The Hook Embrace,"** is a humanoid predatory anomaly standing approximately **2.8 meters** tall. Its body consists of pale flesh stretched over an unnaturally thin frame concealed beneath a hanging cloak of living tissue. Suspended from nearly every fold of this membrane are hundreds of fleshy hooks composed of hardened keratin and an unidentified organic material.     

The entity possesses two elongated forelimbs measuring over three meters each, allowing it to seize prey from surprising distances.    

Despite its horrifying appearance, LD-086 is physically fragile. Standard firearms are highly effective against it when vital areas are struck.    

Its danger lies almost entirely in allowing it to close the distance.    

---     

## Discovery   

LD-086 was discovered following a series of disappearances around several abandoned slaughterhouses and rural barns across **[REDACTED]**.    

Recovered security footage consistently showed victims being pulled into darkness by dozens of moving hooks before disappearing from view.    

DIVIDE recovery teams successfully terminated three specimens before capturing a fourth alive for study.    
 
Evidence suggests multiple individuals of the species exist.    

---    

## Physical Characteristics    

The creature moves slowly while searching for prey.    

Once a target is detected, it silently extends its elongated arms before launching clusters of hooks with alarming speed.      

The hooks are attached to strands of living tissue and remain connected to the entity.    
 
Autopsies revealed the hooks continuously regenerate throughout the creature's life.    

---    

## Anomalous Properties     

### Living Hooks     

The hooks penetrate clothing, armor, and flesh with remarkable ease.    

Each embedded hook contracts toward LD-086 with increasing force.    
 
Attempts to pull free usually result in additional tissue damage rather than escape.    

The more hooks attached to a victim, the stronger the pulling force becomes.    

Victims frequently become immobilized before reaching the creature.    

---    

### The Embrace    

Once prey is pulled within arm's reach, LD-086 wraps its flesh-covered membrane around the victim.     

Thousands of additional hooks immediately penetrate the body from every direction, locking the victim inside.    

Internal microphones have recorded prolonged screaming lasting several minutes before silence.    

Recovered remains suggest victims are dissolved and absorbed by unknown biological processes occurring within the membrane.     

---      

## Behavior    

LD-086 is primarily nocturnal.    
 
It favors abandoned:    

* Barns   
* Slaughterhouses   
* Butcher shops   
* Livestock processing facilities    
* Remote storage buildings   

The entity remains perfectly motionless for hours before suddenly attacking anything entering its territory.     

It rarely pursues prey beyond approximately one hundred meters from its nesting location.     

---    

## Weaknesses     

Unlike many anomalous predators, LD-086 is remarkably easy to kill.      

Recommended engagement protocol:     

* Maintain distance.    
* Never allow physical contact.    
* Use buckshot whenever possible.    
* Prioritize the chest cavity or head.    

Shotguns are considered the ideal weapon due to the creature's thin frame and numerous exposed organs.    

Standard rifles and pistols are also effective with accurate fire.     

Incendiary weapons are unnecessary.    

---   

## Containment     

LD-086 is housed inside a reinforced agricultural simulation chamber resembling the abandoned structures where it naturally nests. All tho there are multiple instances still outside breeding.   

The chamber contains no organic matter other than the entity itself.   

Automated feeding systems eliminate the need for direct personnel interaction.    

Personnel entering the containment area must operate in teams of no fewer than four members.    

---    

## Threat Assessment    

Although classified only as **Class II**, LD-086 has an unusually high fatality rate among untrained civilians.    

Most deaths occur because victims underestimate its speed after the first hook lands.    

Researchers emphasize that **surviving the initial attack almost always results in survival**, while allowing multiple hooks to attach drastically reduces escape probability.    

---    

## Survival Notice   

> **If you feel something catch your clothing in the dark...**     
>
> **Do not pull.**     
>
> **Run toward light while cutting the connecting tissue.**      
>
> **If you see the membrane opening, it is already too late.**
