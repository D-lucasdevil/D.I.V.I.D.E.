## Overview   

LD-079, designated **"The Crimson Witness,"** is a humanoid female anomaly believed to be one of the oldest entities currently known to DIVIDE.   

The anomaly presents as a woman with pale gray skin, dark hair, and a singular golden eye often concealed behind her hands. Numerous witnesses report that the number and position of her eyes change between observations.   

LD-079 displays no consistent hostility, no consistent benevolence, and no recognizable morality.   

The entity has repeatedly described itself as:   

> "The final witness."   

No explanation has been provided.

## Physical Description   

LD-079 appears physically human.   

Medical scans produce contradictory results.   

Different scans have reported:   

* No organs.   
* Multiple hearts.   
* Infinite internal depth.    
* Complete absence of biological structures.    

Autopsies have been attempted.   

All failed.   

The anomaly remains alive regardless of damage.    

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### The Eye Beyond   

The golden eye visible behind LD-079's hands is not believed to be a physical organ.   

Personnel who maintain eye contact for extended periods report:   

* Loss of memory.   
* Personality alteration.   
* Severe paranoia.    
* Religious obsession.    
* Complete psychological collapse.    

Survivors frequently describe:    

> "Something looking back."    

---   

### Rage Manifestation    

LD-079 rarely displays emotion.   

However, when sufficiently angered, reality surrounding the anomaly begins to deteriorate.   

Documented effects include:    

* Buildings twisting into impossible shapes.   
* Matter transforming into biological tissue.    
* Localized gravitational collapse.    
* Spontaneous appearance of eyes on surrounding surfaces.     

Researchers designated this state:    

## Crimson Rage Event   

The longest recorded event lasted 47 seconds.   

It resulted in the permanent loss of an entire containment sector.    

---    

### Judgment    

The anomaly can apparently choose a target and issue what researchers call a **Judgment.**    

Effects vary dramatically.    

Known outcomes include:    

* Instant death.    
* Complete erasure from memory.   
* Sudden aging.    
* Transformation into inert objects.    
* Permanent unconsciousness.    

No pattern has been identified.    

---    

### Authority Over Fear    

LD-079 naturally emits a passive effect causing emotional instability.    

Personnel near the anomaly often experience:    

* Guilt.   
* Terror.   
* Shame.   
* Existential dread.   

The anomaly appears capable of amplifying specific emotions at will.    

In one incident, a hostile operative became so overwhelmed by regret that he surrendered despite being physically unharmed.    

---   

### The Red Hands    

The hands covering LD-079's face are anomalous constructs.    

They have been observed:    

* Intercepting projectiles.   
* Blocking attacks.    
* Moving independently.    
* Appearing in locations separate from the anomaly.    

The hands cannot currently be damaged.    

---    

## Discovery    

LD-079 was not discovered.    

According to recovered records, the anomaly simply appeared inside a Director meeting.     

Security footage shows:    

1. The chair was empty.    
2. One frame later LD-079 was seated.    
3. Nobody present noticed her arrival.    

The anomaly remained silent for approximately 18 minutes.    

Before disappearing, she stated:   

> "You are building walls around storms."    

No explanation was given.   

---   

## Incident 079-Prime   

A Class IV anomaly attempted to attack LD-079 during a breach.   

Witnesses describe the following:    

* LD-079 looked toward the entity.   
* The entity stopped moving.    
* All surveillance systems failed.    
* A loud sound resembling distant screaming was recorded so high pitched it broke all audio equipment.    

When visual contact resumed, the Class IV anomaly was gone.    

No remains were recovered.   

No energy spike was detected.    

No known anomalous ability matches the event.    

The official report simply states:    

> "Target no longer existed."   


## Containment    

No successful containment procedure exists.   

LD-079 appears and disappears at will.    

Attempts to track, monitor, or restrict the entity have universally failed.   

The anomaly generally ignores personnel unless directly addressed.
