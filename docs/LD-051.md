**File: LD-051 — “Remnants of Ash”**  

**Designation: Dormant Thermal Golem / Item-Type Awakening Anomaly**  
**Threat Level: Class III**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 5+**  
**Containment Status: Secured — Antarctic Cryo-Containment Active**  


![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HI7TsdBWQAAVRRh?format=jpg&name=large)  



**Overview:**  

LD-051, designated “Remnants of Ash,” is a dormant anomalous entity primarily existing as a massive pile of ash, coal, and scorched remains centered around an indestructible humanoid skull.  

Despite its inactive appearance, LD-051 is capable of entering an awakened state under specific thermal conditions, transforming into a highly destructive humanoid entity driven entirely by nearby heat sources.  

**Dormant Form:**  

In its inactive state, LD-051 appears as:  

A large mound of:  
Ash  
Coal  
Burned organic residue  
Centered around a:  
Blackened humanoid skull exhibiting severe thermal damage  

**The skull itself is:**  

Completely indestructible by known conventional means  
Believed to function as the anomaly’s core anchor  

**Important:**  

The skull is not equivalent to a biological head  
Damage to it does not visibly affect awakened behavior  
Activation Conditions:  

LD-051 cannot awaken naturally.  

**Awakening requires:**  

Presence of sufficient coal-based fuel  
Ambient temperature reaching or exceeding:  

145.15°C  

within an approximate 5 km radius  

**Awakened State:**  

Upon activation:  

Ash rapidly forms:  
Musculature  
Cartilage-like structures  
Coal compresses into:  
Skeletal framework  
Entity assumes a:  
Random humanoid appearance during each manifestation  
Thermal Output:  

Internal body temperature rapidly exceeds:  

3400°C  

This temperature is maintained for:  

Maximum: 1 hour  
Regardless of additional fuel intake  
Behavior:  

LD-051 demonstrates extremely primitive behavior patterns.  

Primary instinct:  

Seek and consume heat sources.  

**The anomaly:**  

Ignores pain  
Does not evade attacks  
Does not utilize tactics  
Will directly advance toward:  
Heat  
Fire  
Energy output  
Active weapon systems  
Regeneration:  

If damaged:  

Detached ash and coal fragments are re-attracted to the body  
Partial reconstruction occurs rapidly  

**However:**  

Destruction of the primary body mass immediately forces:  
Instant dormancy  
Collapse back into ash state  

A sufficiently powerful precision strike:  

**Example:**  
Railgun impact  
Can disable LD-051 if the core structure is destroyed before regeneration stabilizes  
Reactivation Conditions:  

If reduced fully to dormant state:  

Only the skull remains active  
Reactivation again requires:  
Coal-based material  
145.15°C minimum temperature threshold  

Spontaneous revival:  

Impossible  

**Containment Protocol:**  

LD-051 is contained in a reinforced cryogenic chamber beneath Antarctica.  

Containment measures include:  

Constant flooding with:  
Liquid nitrogen  
Multi-layer thermal suppression systems  
Zero combustible material permitted nearby  
Incident Record — Initial Manifestation:  

LD-051 first awakened following an unidentified Pulse event.  

The anomaly advanced toward multiple industrial heat sources, causing widespread destruction through:  

Extreme heat  
Environmental ignition  
Structural melting  

Neutralization required:  

**Deployment of:**  
ECHO-9 long-range containment unit  

A precision anti-material strike successfully forced premature dormancy.  

Additional Behavioral Trigger:  

Testing revealed that LD-051 reacts aggressively to the spoken word:  

“Neo”  

Reason:  

Unknown  

Response:  

Temporary agitation  
Increased thermal activity  

**However:**  

Without nearby heat sources, the anomaly calms rapidly and returns dormant.  
Threat Assessment:  

**LD-051 remains classified as Class III due to:**  

Predictable behavior  
Limited activation requirements  
Hard time limit on awakened state  
Vulnerability to sufficient precision firepower  

Despite extreme temperatures, the anomaly:  

Is not intelligent  
Is not unstoppable  
Can be disabled under proper engagement conditions  
**Internal Memorandum:**  

“It’s not alive.  
It’s a wildfire trying to become a person.”  

— DIVIDE Thermal Research Division
