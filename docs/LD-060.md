# File: LD-060 — “The Timekeeper’s Pocket Watch”  

**Designation:** Temporal/Reality-Altering Object  
**Threat Level:** Class IV  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+  
**Containment Status:** Lunar Isolation Containment Active  

---  

## Overview  

LD-060, designated **“The Timekeeper’s Pocket Watch,”** is an anomalous golden pocket watch believed capable of manipulating localized temporal flow.  

The anomaly was discovered within a large impact crater approximately:  

## 150 kilometers from Athens, Greece  

At the time of recovery, LD-060 emitted extreme thermal energy estimated at:  

## ~1000°C  

Due to the hazardous temperatures, the DIVIDE thermal recovery unit:  

## IHD-αΩ (since than disbanded due to extermination of the team)  

was deployed for extraction.  

Approximately five days after recovery, the anomaly’s temperature abruptly dropped to:  

## 0°C  

without any observable cooling process.  

Initial testing began shortly afterward.  

---  

## Physical Description  

LD-060 resembles an ornate antique pocket watch constructed from unidentified metallic materials.  

Observed characteristics include:  

* Gold-like outer casing  
* Platinum-colored clock hands  
* No visible manufacturer markings  
* No detectable mechanical movement despite functioning internally  

An inscription engraved on the rear surface reads:  

> “Ο χρόνος κυλάει”  

Translated from Greek:  

> “Time flies.”  

Material analysis has repeatedly failed to identify the exact composition of the object.  

---  

## Initial Testing Incident  

The first authorized interaction involved an assigned test operative instructed to:  

* Touch the clock hands  
* Pull the watch’s adjustment pin  

Immediately after activation, the operative collapsed with multiple fractures in both hands despite no external physical force being observed.  

Upon regaining consciousness, the operative stated:  

> “Everything went grey. Nothing was moving except me. Then suddenly everything went black… and I woke up here.”  

Following this event, all direct experimentation was suspended indefinitely.  

---  

## Suspected Properties  

While the complete capabilities of LD-060 remain unknown, researchers believe the anomaly possesses some degree of:  

* Time manipulation  
* Temporal suspension  
* Chronological distortion  

Theoretical projections suggest a sufficiently skilled user could potentially:  

* Stop localized time  
* Accelerate time flow  
* Rewind short temporal intervals  
* Alter movement perception relative to surrounding environments  

These theories remain unconfirmed due to the prohibition of further testing.  

---  

## Destruction Attempts  

Following the initial incident, immediate destruction of LD-060 was authorized.  

A total of:  

## 9,843 destruction attempts  

were conducted using:  

* Extreme heat  
* Cryogenic exposure  
* High-yield explosives  
* Electromagnetic destabilization  
* Anomalous weapon systems  

All attempts failed.  

No measurable damage has ever been inflicted upon LD-060.  

---  

## Containment History  

Following failed destruction efforts, LD-060 was placed under maximum security lockdown.  

Initial containment involved storage within:  

## “The Time-Proof Vault”  

a specialized anti-temporal containment chamber.  

However, containment failed when the vault doors became permanently unable to close while LD-060 remained inside.  

Researchers later concluded the anomaly was interfering with localized mechanical timing systems.  

Due to escalating containment concerns, DIVIDE authorized construction of a dedicated lunar containment facility.  

LD-060 is currently stored:  

* Beneath the lunar surface  
* Inside a reinforced temporal isolation chamber  
* Under continuous automated surveillance  

Access is restricted exclusively to personnel possessing:  

## Full DIVIDE Authorization  

---  

## Last Recorded Interaction  

The last confirmed direct interaction with LD-060 occurred on:  

## 06/04/2009  

Following this event, all physical contact was permanently prohibited.  

No personnel have interacted directly with the anomaly since.  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-060 is classified as:  

## **Class IV**  

Reasoning:  

* Potential large-scale temporal manipulation  
* Impossible destruction status  
* Unknown upper capability limits  
* Severe containment uncertainty  

Although the anomaly has not demonstrated openly hostile behavior, the theoretical implications of uncontrolled temporal distortion are considered catastrophic.  

Researchers universally agree:  

## LD-060 represents one of DIVIDE’s highest-priority containment risks.
