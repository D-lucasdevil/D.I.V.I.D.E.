# File: LD-072 — "The Finger Maiden"    

**Designation:** Humanoid Parasitic Anomaly    

**Threat Level:** Class II    

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+    

**Containment Status:** Standard Humanoid Containment    

---    

## Overview    

LD-072, designated **"The Finger Maiden,"** is a humanoid female anomaly measuring approximately 1.5 meters in height.   

The entity resembles a severely malnourished young woman with pale skin, dark hair, and an unnaturally wide smile exposing dozens of irregular teeth.  

Most notably, LD-072's body is composed almost entirely of living finger-like growths.   

These structures replace:   

* Musculature   
* Internal organs    
* Portions of skeletal tissue    

Autopsies are impossible due to rapid regeneration of damaged tissue.   

The finger growths remain active even after separation from the main body.    

---    

## Physical Appearance    

The entity's torso appears swollen and layered with thousands of intertwined fingers.    

Many of these fingers:    

* Twitch independently.   
* Bend in impossible directions.    
* Continue growing after removal.   

Researchers estimate the total number of fingers present inside LD-072 exceeds 40,000.    

The exact number is impossible to determine due to continuous growth.    

---    

## Behavior    

LD-072 demonstrates limited intelligence.    

It is capable of speech but rarely engages in conversation.    

Most vocalizations consist of:    

> "Yours are prettier."   

> "I need more."   

> "Give me your fingers."    

The anomaly displays extreme fixation on human hands and feet.   

Individuals with missing digits appear to interest it less.   

---   

## Hunting Behavior   

LD-072 avoids direct confrontation whenever possible.   

Instead, it prefers:   

* Ventilation systems   
* Crawlspaces   
* Basements   
* Dark corridors   
* Storage rooms   

The entity will stalk a target for extended periods before attacking.   

---   

## Finger Harvesting   

Upon successful capture of a victim:   

LD-072 attempts to remove all:   

* Fingers   
* Toes    

using sharpened fingernail-like structures.   

Victims are usually kept alive throughout the procedure.   

The removed digits are consumed immediately.    

---   

## Parasitic Replacement   

After harvesting digits, LD-072 implants its own finger growths into the victim.    

Replacement locations include:    

### Standard Locations   

* Hands   
* Feet   

### Advanced Cases   

* Chest   
* Neck   
* Back   
* Face   

### Severe Cases   

* Tongue   
* Throat   
* Mouth   
* Nasal passages   

The implanted fingers remain alive and continue growing.   

---  

## Progressive Infection   

Victims experience multiple stages:   

### Stage One   

* Minor finger growth.   
* Tingling sensation.   

### Stage Two   

* Additional fingers emerge from skin.   
* Loss of fine motor control.   

### Stage Three   

* Internal finger growths begin replacing tissue.    
* Difficulty speaking.    
* Difficulty swallowing.   

### Stage Four   

* Complete biological conversion.    

At this stage the victim becomes:    

### LD-072-A   

A secondary finger colony controlled by LD-072.    

---   

## Physical Capabilities    

Despite its disturbing nature, LD-072 is physically weak.   

Observed limitations:   

* Fragile bones.   
* Human-level strength.    
* Poor durability.   

The anomaly has been successfully neutralized by:   

* Firearms   
* Blunt weapons   
* Security personnel   

However:   

Destruction of the main body does not eliminate existing infections.   

---   

## Incident Report 072-04   

A maintenance worker became separated from his team for approximately 11 minutes.   

Upon recovery:   

* All fingers had been removed.   
* Forty-seven additional fingers were present across his chest.   
* Eleven fingers were discovered growing inside his mouth.    

The victim repeatedly stated:     

> "She said mine weren't enough."    

Conversion reached Stage Three before emergency amputation procedures halted progression.    

---    

## Containment Procedures   

LD-072 is housed inside a reinforced humanoid containment chamber.   

Requirements:   

* Weekly incineration of excess finger growth.    
* Continuous surveillance.   
* No personnel permitted to enter alone.   
 
Emergency protocol:    

If unexplained fingers are discovered growing inside a facility:   

1. Quarantine affected area.   
2. Search for hidden finger clusters.   
3. Assume LD-072 has escaped containment.   
 
---   

## Threat Assessment   

LD-072 is classified:    

### Class II    

Reasoning:   

* Extremely disturbing infection mechanism.   
* Capable of creating secondary victims.   
* Difficult to detect during early stages.    

However:   

* Physically fragile.   
* Limited mobility.   
* No large-scale destructive capabilities.   

Early intervention remains highly effective.
