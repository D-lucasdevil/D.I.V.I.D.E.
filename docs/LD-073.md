# File: LD-073 — "The Watchers"  

**Designation:** Autonomous Aerial Anomaly  

**Threat Level:** Class II  

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 2+  

**Containment Status:** Contained  



![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HKJrfTGWYAAb0HP?format=jpg&name=large)




---  

## Overview  

LD-073, designated **"The Watchers,"** consists of four floating spherical entities resembling large black eyes.  

Each entity measures approximately 30 centimeters in diameter and possesses:  

* A black sclera  
* Bright yellow iris  
* Vertical pupil  

No visible biological structures have been detected.  

The entities continuously hover without any observable propulsion system.   

When inactive, the four eyes move together in a precise circular formation around a central point.  

The formation remains identical regardless of environmental conditions.  

---  

## Discovery  

LD-073 was discovered after multiple reports from hikers in northern Romania.  

Witnesses reported:  

* Glowing yellow lights in forests  
* Invisible impacts breaking tree branches  
* Strange "guardian spirits" protecting lost children  

A DIVIDE investigation located the four entities circling an injured teenager.  

The entities became highly aggressive toward recovery personnel until the teenager was evacuated safely.  

Containment was achieved three days later.  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-073 displays signs of limited intelligence.  

The entities appear capable of:  

* Recognizing individuals  
* Identifying threats  
* Forming preferences  

The exact criteria used to determine these preferences remain unknown.  

Some personnel are ignored entirely.  

Others become immediate targets.   

A small number are seemingly adopted by the anomaly.   

Repeated testing has failed to identify a pattern.   

---   

## Aggressive Response   

When LD-073 dislikes an individual, the entities become visibly agitated.   

Warning signs include:   

* Faster circling   
* Increased brightness    
* Simultaneous fixation on the target    

If the target remains nearby, the eyes will launch themselves at extreme speed.  

Impacts have resulted in:

* Broken bones  
* Internal injuries  
* Concussions  
* Structural damage  

Despite this, the entities possess relatively low durability and can be destroyed by conventional weapons.   

---   

## Protective Behavior   

When LD-073 forms an attachment to an individual, behavior changes dramatically.   

The entities begin:  

* Circling the subject constantly  
* Following them throughout the facility   
* Positioning themselves between perceived threats and the subject   

Any threat identified by LD-073 is attacked immediately.   

This includes:   

* Hostile anomalies  
* Armed personnel  
* Security systems   
* Environmental hazards    

Several incidents have shown the entities shielding favored individuals from gunfire by physically intercepting projectiles.    

---   

## Regeneration    

Destroyed instances of LD-073 do not remain destroyed permanently.   

After approximately 30 to 60 days:   

A replacement eye manifests inside the containment chamber.    

The new instance appears identical to the original.   

The source of this regeneration remains unknown.    

---   

## Incident Report 073-07   

Researcher M. Novak entered the containment chamber for routine observation.   

Within three seconds:   

* All four eyes accelerated toward him.   
* Both arms were fractured.   
* The researcher was thrown approximately six meters.     

Two days later, Maintenance Technician Elena Varga entered the chamber after accidentally opening the wrong door.   

LD-073 immediately surrounded her.   

For the next four months, the entities followed her throughout the facility and attacked multiple anomalies that approached her.   

No explanation has been found.    

---   

## Containment Procedures   

LD-073 is contained within a reinforced observation chamber.    

Requirements include:    

* No unnecessary personnel access.    
* Remote monitoring preferred.    
* Favored personnel prohibited from entering containment.    

If attachment occurs:   

The affected individual must be reassigned away from active containment zones until the entities lose interest. if they dont lose interest killing the anomalies tends to nullify attachment but you need to kill all 4     

---    

## Threat Assessment    

LD-073 is classified    
 
### Class II    

Reasoning:    

* Capable of causing severe injury.  
* Unpredictable targeting behavior.   
* Difficult containment due to selective aggression.   

However:   

* Limited offensive capability.   
* Small operational range.   
* Vulnerable to conventional weapons.    

Without a favored target to defend, the anomaly poses only a moderate threat.
