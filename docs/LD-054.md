# **File: LD-054 — “The Sailor’s Lullaby”**  

**Designation:** Deep-Sea Spatial Anomaly / Maritime Predator  
**Threat Level:** **Class III**  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Passive Monitoring Active  

---

# **Overview**  

LD-054, designated **“The Sailor’s Lullaby,”** is a colossal deep-sea anomaly resembling a distorted anglerfish-like organism capable of creating localized spatial distortions within its digestive system.  

Unlike previous assessments, LD-054 is **not considered a global extinction-level entity** due to:  

* Extremely low activity frequency  
* Passive behavior patterns  
* Geographic limitations  
* Predictable feeding cycles  

The anomaly is now classified as:  

> **Class III**  

---

# **Physical Description**  

LD-054 resembles a massive abyssal marine organism combining characteristics of:  

* Anglerfish  
* Deep-sea bloop-type fauna  
* Unknown leviathan species  

Estimated dimensions:  

* Approximate body length:  

  * 300 meters  
* Maximum detected width:  

  * 1000 meters including body mass and lure structure  

The lure (“second moon” phenomenon) possesses:  

* Estimated surface area:  

  * 37,900 square meters  

When active near the surface:  

* The lure emits pale reflected light resembling:  

  * A second moon above the ocean horizon  

---

# **Behavior**  

LD-054 spends the overwhelming majority of its existence:  

* Dormant beneath deep oceanic trenches  
* Motionless  
* Non-aggressive toward ships unless entering feeding zones  

Manifestations occur only:  

* Every several years  
* In isolated oceanic regions  

Recorded appearances:  

* Pacific Ocean near Hawaii  
* Mediterranean waters between Italy and Greece  

---

# **Feeding Cycle**  

LD-054 appears to feed infrequently.  

Rather than hunting aggressively, the anomaly:  

* Lures vessels toward itself  
* Creates disorientation phenomena  
* Swallows nearby targets whole  

The organism then enters:  

> Extended dormant state  

Current estimates suggest:  

* One feeding event sustains inactivity for multiple years ussualy 12 years  

---

# **Internal Spatial Phenomenon**  

The interior of LD-054 does not appear biologically consistent with normal anatomy.  

Victims swallowed by the anomaly enter:  

> A contained extradimensional environment  

This space resembles:  

* Endless coastline  
* Artificial tropical atmosphere  
* Infinite staircase extending into the sky  

---

# **Associated Entities**  

## **LD-054-A — “Simon”**  

A translucent humanoid male entity appearing near shoreline environments within the extradimensional space.  

Characteristics:  

* Calm demeanor  
* White glowing eyes  
* Semi-transparent body  

Behavior:  

* Claims to be sailing toward Hawaii  
* Repeats fragmented phrases and nautical dialogue  
* Does not display direct hostility  

---

## **LD-054-B — “Stella”**  

A female spectral entity appearing intermittently within forested areas of the extradimensional zone.  

Traits:  

* Blue dress featuring:  

  * Stella octangula symbol  
* Appears deceased during some cycles  
* Reappears alive during others  

Believed to exist in:  

> Repeating death-revival state  

---

## **LD-054-C — “The Erlking”**  

A secondary predatory entity occasionally manifesting within forest regions of the extradimensional environment.  

Description:  

* Mounted humanoid silhouette  
* Audible horse galloping precedes manifestation  
* Appears only during darkness cycles  

The Erlking is believed responsible for:  

* Majority of fatalities within LD-054’s internal space  

Unlike earlier assessments:  

> The Erlking is no longer classified as a separate Class IV entity.  

Current assessment:  

* Dangerous localized predator  
* Avoidable under correct conditions  

---

# **Incident Record**  

Initial DIVIDE investigation began following civilian reports of:  

* “Two moons” visible near Hawaii  
* Massive unidentified oceanic roars  

The first expedition was lost after:  

* Vessel experienced upward displacement phenomenon  
* Crew reported water remaining beneath vessel despite aerial motion  

Only survivor:  

> Team Leader Anthony Phillip  

Phillip later died from catastrophic injuries.  

---

# **Second Recorded Expedition**  

AT-Class retrieval teams entered LD-054 intentionally.  

Bodycam footage confirmed:  

* Presence of extradimensional shoreline  
* Interaction with Simon  
* Infinite staircase phenomenon  

After attempted staircase ascent:  
Simon stated:  

> “Bye, Hi, Sigh, Hawaii.”  

Immediately afterward:  

* All visual feeds terminated  
* Personnel declared deceased  
* Bodies unrecovered  

---

# **Weaknesses & Limitations**  

LD-054 is considerably less dangerous than previously believed due to several major limitations:  

### The anomaly:  

* Rarely surfaces  
* Does not actively pursue humanity  
* Remains geographically isolated  
* Requires proximity for predation  
* Cannot rapidly relocate  

Additionally:  

* Internal entities are restricted to the extradimensional space  
* No confirmed inland manifestation has occurred  

---

# **Neutralization Potential**  

While conventional weaponry is ineffective:  

Testing projections suggest:  

* Nuclear bombardment  
* Deep-sea pressure collapse weaponry  
* Large-scale thermal shock systems  

could severely damage or potentially terminate LD-054.  

Due to current low casualty rates:  

> Neutralization has not been approved.  

---

# **Threat Assessment**  

LD-054 is now classified:  

> **Class III**  

Reasoning:  

* Infrequent activity  
* Predictable dormant cycles  
* Limited hunting range  
* No confirmed civilization-ending capability  

Despite terrifying scale:  

> The anomaly behaves more like a territorial deep-sea predator than an active extinction threat.
