# File: LD-075 — "Phantom Edge"   

**Designation:** DIVIDE Anomalous Weapon Platform  

**Threat Level:** Class II   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+    

**Containment Status:** Active Service / Restricted Armory   




![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HKzcu32XwAA5knq?format=jpg&name=medium)  



---   

## Overview    

LD-075, designated **"Phantom Edge,"** is the collective designation for a series of anomalous energy blades developed by DIVIDE for use by the **PHANTOM Squad**.   

Unlike conventional swords, LD-075 possesses no physical blade. Instead, the weapon generates a highly concentrated plasma-electric edge extending from a reinforced katana-style hilt. The resulting blade resembles solid blue-white lightning and emits a low humming sound while active.    

As of writing, **27 instances** of LD-075 exist.   

LD-075 was the **second anomalous weapon platform ever successfully created by DIVIDE**, predating many modern containment-combat technologies.   

While highly effective against biological targets, LD-075 is  weaker than LD-074 "Dominance" and was designed primarily for stealth operations rather than direct engagements.    

---    

## Physical Description    

Each LD-075 unit consists of:   

* Reinforced titanium-composite katana hilt   
* Anomalous power core   
* Energy projection assembly   
* User synchronization matrix   

When inactive, LD-075 appears identical to a standard  katana handle.   

When activated, a blade of concentrated electrical-plasma energy extends approximately 90 centimeters from the hilt.   

The blade emits:   

* Blue-white light   
* Electrical discharge    
* Minimal heat signatures   

Despite appearances, the blade cannot effortlessly melt through large amounts of metal.   

Researchers note that popular media comparisons to "lightsabers" are inaccurate.   

---   

## Origin   

LD-075 was developed during **Project PHANTOM**.   

The weapons were created using biological and anomalous materials recovered from **[REDACTED]**, a deceased anomaly whose remains demonstrated unusual energy-conduction properties.    

Due to the rarity of these materials, only 27 functional units have ever been produced.    

DIVIDE researchers estimate no additional units can be manufactured without obtaining new source material.    

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### User Synchronization   

Every LD-075 forms a permanent bond with a designated operator.    

Once synchronized:    

* Only the linked user can activate the blade.   
* The weapon responds instantly to their commands.   
* Synchronization cannot be transferred voluntarily.   

Researchers have been unable to determine the exact mechanism responsible.    

---    

### Blade Generation   

Upon activation, LD-075 produces a blade composed of highly concentrated electrical energy.   

The blade is capable of:   

* Cutting flesh with ease   
* Severing light armor   
* Damaging weaker anomalies   
* Disabling electronics   

The blade is significantly less effective against:    

* Heavy armor   
* Reinforced bunkers   

---   

### Thermal Cauterization   

Any wound inflicted by LD-075 is instantly cauterized.    

Effects include:   

* Near-total elimination of bleeding    
* Reduced forensic evidence   
* Lower likelihood of target detection    

This property makes LD-075 highly desirable during infiltration and assassination missions.   

---    

### Death of the User   

When a synchronized user dies:   

* The weapon immediately deactivates.   
* Future activation attempts fail.   
* The synchronization matrix becomes inert.   

An inactive LD-075 is considered permanently unusable.   

Recovered units are dismantled and recycled for parts whenever possible.   

---   

## Exceptional Incident 075-01   

One confirmed exception exists.   

Following the death of PHANTOM Operative P-02, his assigned LD-075 remained inactive for approximately three weeks.   

During a civilian evacuation event, the weapon unexpectedly synchronized with an untrained civilian.   

The individual activated the blade immediately without prior exposure.   

After extensive testing confirmed compatibility, the civilian was recruited into PHANTOM Squad.   

Three years later they would become the squad's commanding officer.   

No explanation for the event has been discovered.   

---   

## Deployment   

LD-075 units are primarily assigned to:    

### PHANTOM Squad   

Specializing in:   

* Covert elimination   
* Reconnaissance   
* Counter-anomaly operations   
* Blacksite infiltration   

PHANTOM personnel consider LD-075 their signature weapon platform.   

---   

## Containment Procedures   

Inactive LD-075 instances are stored in secured armories.   

Active units remain assigned to authorized personnel.   

Any lost unit must be recovered immediately.   

If an active user is killed during deployment, retrieval teams are dispatched to recover the inert weapon.    

---   

## Threat Assessment    

LD-075 is classified:    

### Class II   

Reasons:   

* Limited offensive capability compared to higher-tier anomalies.   
* Requires synchronization.   
* Cannot function independently.   
* Effective primarily as a weapon rather than a self-sustaining threat.   

Despite this classification, trained operators can use LD-075 with extreme efficiency.
