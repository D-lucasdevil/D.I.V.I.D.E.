# File: LD-083 — "Hellbreaker"   

**Designation:** Anomalous Breaching Hammer   

**Threat Level:** Class III   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+   

**Containment Status:** Active Service — BESERK Armory   









---   

## Overview   

LD-083, designated **"Hellbreaker,"** is the third successful anomalous weapon engineered entirely by DIVIDE's Applied Anomalous Engineering Division.   

At first glance, LD-083 resembles an oversized steel breaching hammer fitted with a reinforced shotgun assembly inside the striking head. Unlike conventional firearms, the weapon converts virtually any ammunition loaded into its internal chamber into a unique anomalous incendiary buckshot upon discharge.     

The weapon was specifically designed for **BESERK Squad**, whose operators specialize in close-quarters combat against heavily armored anomalies.    

---    

## Development    

Following the success of **LD-074** and **LD-075**, researchers sought to create a weapon capable of overwhelming durable Class III entities without sacrificing the raw impact force expected from BESERK operatives.    

The result was LD-083.     

Unlike previous DIVIDE weapons, Hellbreaker was never intended for precision.    

Its purpose is simple:    

**Hit as hard as possible.**     

---    

## Physical Description    

LD-083 stands approximately **118 cm** tall and weighs anywhere between **8 kilograms and over 200 kilograms**, depending entirely on the individual wielding it.     

Its head contains:     

* Reinforced breaching hammer faces    
* Internal anomalous combustion chamber    
* Adaptive ammunition converter    
* Pressure release vents    
* Auto-reset firing mechanism   

Despite its appearance, no known manufacturer markings exist.    

---    

## Adaptive Mass    

One of Hellbreaker's most unusual properties is its variable weight.    

When grasped, the weapon instantly analyzes the wielder's physical capabilities.     

Weak individuals experience the hammer as surprisingly manageable.      

Exceptionally strong operators experience dramatically increased mass.       

This increase directly affects:      
 
* Impact force    
* Momentum    
* Recoil energy   
* Shotgun blast intensity     

Researchers describe the phenomenon as:    

> "The stronger you are, the heavier Hellbreaker chooses to become."    

Because of this, no two operators experience the weapon the same way.      

---     

## Anomalous Shot    

Whenever the hammer successfully strikes a solid object, the internal shotgun assembly automatically fires.     

Unlike conventional shotguns, Hellbreaker converts **any compatible ammunition** into its own proprietary anomalous shell.     

Observed characteristics include:     

* Oversized buckshot pellets.    
* Incendiary combustion on impact.    
* Limited armor penetration.    
* Massive close-range stopping power.    

The flames produced cannot spread naturally and extinguish within several seconds, making the weapon surprisingly safe for indoor breaching operations.     

---    

## Ammunition Conversion     

Testing confirms Hellbreaker accepts nearly every conventional ammunition type.    

Examples include:    

* 9×19mm    
* 5.56 NATO    
* 7.62×39mm    
* .50 BMG    
* Birdshot   
* Slugs   

Regardless of input, the weapon always converts ammunition into standardized incendiary buckshot.    

No explanation for this conversion currently exists.    

---    

## Reload Mechanism    

Hellbreaker stores enough anomalous energy for **20 successful strikes** before requiring manual reloading.     

Reloading consumes **two standard shotgun shells** regardless of ammunition originally loaded.     

The process requires approximately **four seconds**.      

No jams have ever been recorded.        

---      

## Combat Performance     

Against armored targets, Hellbreaker combines:     

* Crushing kinetic impact.    
* Simultaneous incendiary buckshot.    
* High stagger potential.    

This dual-impact design allows operators to break through both armor and regenerative tissue far more efficiently than conventional melee weapons.     

However, its relatively slow swing speed prevents it from outperforming faster anomalous weapons in prolonged engagements.     

---   

## Personnel Assignment    

LD-083 is issued almost exclusively to **BESERK Squad** due to:    

* High physical demands.    
* Aggressive combat doctrine.    
* Exceptional effectiveness during breaching operations.    

PHANTOM operators have repeatedly declined authorization, citing excessive noise generation and unnecessary collateral damage.    

---    

## Power Classification    
 
Current weapon ranking:    

* **LD-074** — Strongest DIVIDE-manufactured weapon.    
* **LD-083 "Hellbreaker"** — Second strongest.    
* **LD-075** — Third strongest.    
 
Researchers consider Hellbreaker the ideal balance between destructive capability and operational reliability.
