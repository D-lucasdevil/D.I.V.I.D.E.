# File: LD-080 — "The Marionette"   

**Designation:** Parasitic Neuro-Anomalous Entity   

**Threat Level:** Class II   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 3+   

**Containment Status:** Joint Containment Active   



![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HLzbgarWoAA6juA?format=jpg&name=large)




---   

## Overview   

LD-080 consists of two distinct organisms existing in permanent symbiosis.   

The primary anomaly is an enormous biomechanical parasitic organism designated **LD-080-A**.   

Attached to it is a human female child, designated **LD-080-B**, estimated to be approximately ten years old.   

Evidence strongly suggests the child is **not anomalous**.   

She is considered a victim.    

Attempts to separate the two have consistently failed.    

---    

### LD-080-A     

The primary organism resembles a massive fusion of:    

* Organic tissue    
* Mechanical structures   
* Exposed neural matter    
* Unknown biological components    

Its cranial cavity remains partially transparent, exposing continuously moving brain tissue.    

Hundreds of cables connect directly into LD-080-B's nervous system.    

---    

### LD-080-B   

A physically normal human child.   

Medical examination confirms:    

* No abnormal genetics.   
* Normal physiology.   
* Severe psychological trauma.    

When the parasite is dormant she behaves like an extremely frightened child.    

She rarely speaks above a whisper.    

---    

## Neural Domination    

LD-080-A continuously interfaces with the girl's brain.    

When passive:    

* The child retains partial control.    
* Independent speech is possible.    
* Memory remains intact.    

When resistance is detected, LD-080-A increases neural synchronization.    

During this state:   

* The child's movements become mechanical.   
* Speech changes dramatically.    
* Higher brain functions become suppressed.    

Researchers believe the parasite temporarily overlays portions of its own neural activity onto the child's brain rather than replacing it entirely.    

---   

## Defensive Behavior    

Any attempt to physically interfere with the connection immediately provokes LD-080-A.    

Despite being relatively weak compared to higher-tier anomalies, it becomes highly aggressive when the neural links are threatened.    

Observed defensive abilities include:    

* High-speed mechanical limbs.   
* Bone-piercing tendrils.   
* Powerful hydraulic strikes.   
* Limited biological regeneration.   

---   

## Incident 080-02   

DIVIDE authorized an emergency surgical separation attempt.   

Five minutes into the operation, every neural cable simultaneously contracted.    

LD-080-A entered a defensive state never previously observed.    

The operating theater was destroyed.    

Casualties:    

* 4 Researchers    
* 3 Security Personnel    

The procedure was immediately terminated.    

---    

## Psychological State of LD-080-B    

Following failed separation attempts, the child displayed extreme psychological deterioration.    

Symptoms include:   

* Chronic fear.   
* Dissociation.    
* Night terrors while awake.    
* Repeated pleas not to be left alone with the parasite.    

One recording captured the child quietly asking:    

> "Can you make it sleep... just for one day?"    

No successful method has yet been found.    

---   

## Containment   

LD-080 occupies a reinforced containment suite designed to minimize external stimulation.    

Personnel interaction is limited to remote communication whenever possible.    

Medical care for the child is provided through automated systems.    

All proposals involving lethal termination remain suspended.    

---   

## Research Importance    

Although ethically controversial, LD-080 remains one of DIVIDE's highest-priority neurological research subjects.    

The parasite demonstrates the ability to:   

* Override voluntary movement.   
* Suppress memory.   
* Rewrite emotional responses.    
* Maintain long-term neural synchronization with a host.    

Researchers believe understanding these mechanisms could eventually lead to technologies capable of resisting or countering other mind-affecting anomalies or the majority of population.    

---   

## Threat Assessment   

LD-080 is classified **Class II** because:    

* The parasite is physically vulnerable.    
* It possesses limited offensive capability outside close range.   
* It relies almost entirely on maintaining a host.    

The primary danger lies in its neurological control rather than raw destructive power.
