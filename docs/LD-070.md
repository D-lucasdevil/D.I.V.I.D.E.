# File: LD-070 — "There's A Devil In My Heart"    

**Designation:** Anomalous Component   
**Threat Level:** Class III (Class IV+ if utilized by LD-047)   
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+   
**Containment Status:** Cooperative Storage — Authorized Interaction Only   






---  

## Overview  

LD-070, designated **"There's A Devil In My Heart,"** is an anomalous music player resembling an older MP3 device modified with unknown technology.  

The device possesses five buttons:  

* Play/Pause  
* Fast Forward  
* Rewind  
* Scissor Symbol  
* Chainsaw Symbol  

The final two buttons are anomalous in nature and grant separate enhancement pathways to the user.  

Researchers believe the device was intentionally designed to create temporary hosts for two unknown entities referred to internally as:  

* **The Black Spark**  
* **The Iron Hunger**  

Neither entity has been directly observed.  

---  

## Activation  

When the Play button is pressed:  

* The Scissor and Chainsaw buttons illuminate.  
* The screen displays:  

> NOW PLAYING  

The user must then choose one of the two available paths.  

Only one path may be selected at a time.  

---  

# Path One — The Black Spark  

Selecting the Scissor button causes the display to change to:  

> THE ONE TOUCHED BY THE BLACK SPARK  

The user immediately gains access to anomalous combat abilities.  

---  

### Black Spark Discharges  

The user gains the ability to trigger violent bursts of dark electrical energy during physical strikes.  

Effects include:  

* Massive impact amplification  
* Temporary speed increases  
* Enhanced reaction time  

Repeated usage appears to place increasing strain on the user's body.  

---  

### Fracture Arts  

The user gains access to two spatial cutting techniques:  

#### Divide   

Produces precision cutting attacks capable of severing most materials.  

#### Rend  

Creates wider destructive cuts designed for structural damage.  

Both abilities appear to operate through localized sharpened air slices.

---  

### Physical Enhancement  

Users demonstrate:  

* Increased strength  
* Increased endurance   
* Increased durability  
* Accelerated combat adaptation  

---  

# Path Two — The Iron Hunger  

Selecting the Chainsaw button causes the display to change to:  

> THE ONE WHO FORGOT THEIR NAME  

The user gains access to a second enhancement pathway.  

---  

### Blood Restoration  

Consumption of blood allows rapid recovery from injuries.  

Observed regeneration includes:  

* Organ damage  
* Bone fractures  
* Severe lacerations  

The process is not instantaneous but greatly exceeds normal healing rates.  

---  

### Iron Manifestation  

The user can generate chainsaw-like appendages from any limb.  

These manifestations appear biomechanical in nature.  

Observed locations include:  

* Arms  
* Legs  
* Shoulders  
* Back  

---  

### Chain Projection  

Manifested saws can be launched on chain-like extensions.  

Applications include:  

* Grappling  
* Target capture  
* Mid-range attacks  

---  

## Overload Event  

Both pathways become progressively stronger over time.  

Power increases continue for approximately:  

## 40 Minutes  

Following this threshold, one of two fatal outcomes occurs.  

---  

### Black Spark Path  

A bolt of anomalous dark lightning descends upon the user.  

Death is immediate.  

No survivors have been recorded.  

---  

### Iron Hunger Path  

The user enters a feeding state and forcibly consumes their own heart.  

Death follows within seconds.   

No known intervention prevents the process.  

---  

# Special Interaction — LD-047  

LD-047 displays a unique compatibility with LD-070.  

Unlike normal users:  

* She experiences no Overload Event.  
* Power accumulation continues indefinitely.  
* Both pathways may be activated simultaneously.  

Because of this interaction, LD-070 is currently permitted within supervised proximity of LD-047.  

Researchers note significant improvements in her cooperation levels while in possession of the device.  

---  

## Enhanced State  

After approximately 40 minutes of use, LD-047 gains access to additional abilities.  

### Complete Fracture Arts  

LD-047 gains unrestricted access to advanced cutting techniques.  

Observed effects include:  

* Large-scale severance attacks  
* Multi-target cutting fields  
* Structural disassembly   

---  

### Iron Companion Manifestation  

LD-047 can separate part of the Iron Hunger pathway into an independent entity designated:  

## LD-070-1  

Codename:  

### "The Iron Companion"  

LD-070-1 appears as a hostile humanoid construct possessing chainsaw-based weaponry.  

The entity remains loyal exclusively to LD-047.  

---  

### Fusion State  

LD-047 may merge with LD-070-1.  

This results in:  

* Increased physical power   
* Greater regeneration  
* Expanded weapon manifestations  

Researchers estimate this state approaches lower Class IV threat levels.  

---  

## Containment  

LD-070 remains secured within a reinforced anomalous storage vault.  

Interaction requires:  
 
* Level 6 authorization  
* Medical supervision  
* Emergency response teams on standby  
 
Testing has been significantly reduced following multiple fatal incidents.   

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-070 is classified as:  

# Class III  

Reasoning:  

* Highly lethal enhancement effects  
* Consistent user fatalities  
* Significant combat amplification  

However:  

* Requires voluntary activation  
* Possesses a predictable failure point  
* Limited area of effect  

When utilized by LD-047, temporary threat assessments may rise into Class IV territory yet a low tier IV because the affected area and power is lower compared to others.
