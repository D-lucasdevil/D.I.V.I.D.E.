***File: LD-049 — “RAGE”***  

**Designation: Eldritch-Linked Humanoid / Cognito-Hazard Entity**  
**Threat Level: Class IV — Extreme**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 5+**  
**Containment Status: Contained — HIGH RISK**  



![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HHa0yYaWwAAm-m8?format=png&name=900x900)  





**Overview:**  

LD-049, designated “RAGE,” is a humanoid entity of unknown origin exhibiting strong Lovecraftian / extra-dimensional properties.  

The entity was first discovered through a corrupted surveillance feed labeled:  

“CAM 3 — CTFID Channel 2”  

No record exists of how the recording system was installed or who initiated the capture.  

**Physical Description:**  

LD-049 appears as a small, female humanoid entity:  

Pale, almost corpse-like skin  
Long, disordered black hair  
One visible eye:  
Fully open  
Circular, unnatural pupil  

**Notable anomalies:**  

Multiple worm-like appendages emerge from:  
Eye socket  
Surrounding environment  
Black, root-like growth spreads outward from the entity into surrounding surfaces  
**Primary Anomalous Effects:**  
1. Emotional Amplification Field (“RAGE Effect”)  
Any individual within proximity begins experiencing:  
Rapid emotional escalation  
Irrational anger  
Loss of control  

**Progression:**  

Irritation → Aggression → Violent rage → Total psychological collapse 
2. Cognito-Hazardous Observation  
Viewing LD-049 through:  
Cameras  
Screens  
Recordings  

**Results in:**  

Gradual mental instability  
Auditory hallucinations  
Compulsive fixation  

**Long exposure leads to:**  

Self-harm  
Violent behavior toward others  
3. Environmental Corruption  
**The area around LD-049 becomes:**  
Darkened  
Organic growth begins spreading (vein/root-like structures)  
Worm-like entities manifest:  
Non-biological  
Unknown origin  
Not independently hostile, but psychologically disturbing  
4. “Containment Illusion” Phenomenon  

Despite being physically contained, LD-049 appears to exist in multiple locations simultaneously via recording systems.  

**Surveillance feeds may:**  
Activate without power  
Display LD-049 in areas where it is not physically present  
**Behavior:**  
LD-049 remains physically stationary  
Does not actively pursue targets  
However:  
Indirectly causes extreme violence through emotional manipulation  
Containment Protocol:  
Stored in a sealed, isolated chamber  
NO direct visual observation permitted  
Monitoring only via:  
Filtered, delayed video feed  
AI-sanitized frames  

**Personnel restrictions:**  

Maximum exposure time strictly limited  
Immediate removal upon signs of:  
Agitation  
Emotional instability  
Incident Log — 049-R  

**During initial containment:**  

7 personnel entered chamber  
Within minutes:  
All began exhibiting aggressive behavior  
Result:  
5 fatalities (internal conflict)  
2 survivors (severe psychological damage)  

**The word:**  

“RAGE”  

was later discovered written repeatedly across all recording systems — not physically present in the room.  

**Theoretical Origin:**  
 
LD-049 is believed to be:  

A manifestation of collective human rage  
or  
A fragment of an unknown Lovecraftian entity feeding on emotional states  

**Possibility:**  

Entity is not the source  
But a gateway or anchor point  
**Threat Assessment:**  

Classified as Class IV due to:  

Wide-area psychological impact  
Indirect mass-casualty potential  
Reality/perception interference  
Unknown true extent of influence  
**Internal Memorandum:**

“It doesn’t attack you. 
It makes you attack everything else.”  

— DIVIDE Cognitive Hazard Division
