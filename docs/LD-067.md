# File: LD-067 — “The Attempted Entertainer”  

**Designation:** Sentient Object Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** Class 0  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 1+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained   


![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HJv8JJrWgAAr1NW?format=jpg&name=large)


---  

## Overview  

LD-067, designated **"The Attempted Entertainer,"** is an anomalous mannequin permanently dressed in a stereotypical clown costume.  

The anomaly stands approximately:   

* 1.9 meters tall   
* 32 kilograms in weight  

Despite appearing entirely inanimate, LD-067 is capable of autonomous movement.  

The entity displays no signs of intelligence beyond basic awareness and appears to possess a singular objective:  

**Attempting to be funny.**  

To date, all attempts have failed.  

Researchers unanimously describe the anomaly's performances as:  

* Awkward  
* Confusing  
* Poorly timed  
* Completely unfunny  

Several psychological evaluations have confirmed that exposure to LD-067's attempts at humor causes mild second-hand embarrassment.  

No anomalous effects have been linked to this phenomenon.  

---  

## Physical Description  

LD-067 resembles a standard store mannequin.  

Features include:  

* Painted clown makeup  
* Red artificial nose  
* Oversized clown shoes  
* Patchwork clown outfit  

Material testing suggests the anomaly is composed of ordinary mannequin materials.  

However, all attempts to damage, alter, or destroy LD-067 have failed.  

Documented unsuccessful methods include:  

* Firearms  
* Explosives  
* Industrial crushers  
* Corrosive chemicals  
* High-temperature furnaces  
* Anti-anomaly weapon systems  

Following any attempted destruction, LD-067 simply continues its current activity unaffected.  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-067 constantly seeks opportunities to entertain nearby individuals.  

Observed activities include:  

* Poorly executed juggling  
* Falling over intentionally  
* Attempting magic tricks  
* Telling incomprehensible jokes  
* Performing awkward dances  

When no humans are present, the anomaly continues performing for itself.  

Researchers have noted that LD-067 appears genuinely enthusiastic despite receiving almost universally negative reactions.  

---  

## Primary Anomalous Effect  

LD-067 possesses a single confirmed anomalous ability.  

This effect activates only when an individual:  

* Says "67" while referring to the anomaly  
* Laughs about the number "67"  
* Remembers a joke involving the anomaly and the number "67"  

The anomaly appears capable of detecting these thoughts regardless of distance.  

---  

### Designation Event — 67 Response  

Upon activation:  

1. LD-067 immediately ceases all movement.  
2. The anomaly turns toward the affected individual.  
3. Direct eye contact is established.  

No matter where the affected individual is located on Earth.  

Following activation:  

* All clothing worn by the individual instantly transforms into clown attire.  
* The phrase:  

> "I FIND 67 FUNNY"  

appears engraved across the subject's chest.  

The engraving:  

* Causes no pain  
* Causes no injury  
* Produces no bleeding  
* Cannot be medically removed  

The clown outfit similarly cannot be removed.  

Both effects persist for exactly:  

## Seven Days  

After seven days:  

* The clothing disappears.  
* The engraving vanishes.  
* No physical damage remains.  

---  

## Teleportation  

LD-067 randomly disappears and reappears throughout the planet.  

No pattern has been identified. 

Manifestations have occurred in:  

* Schools  
* Airports  
* Military installations  
* Research facilities  
* Remote wilderness  

The anomaly appears equally confused by these relocations.  

Researchers currently believe the teleportation is involuntary.  

---  

## Containment  

Containment of LD-067 has proven impossible due to its random relocation events.  

Fortunately, containment is considered unnecessary.  

Current protocol consists of:  

* Monitoring reports of clown-related anomalies  
* Recovering observation footage  
* Updating location records when possible  

No active containment teams are assigned. 

---  
 
## Threat Assessment  

LD-067 is classified as:  

# Class 0  

Reasoning:  

* Completely harmless   
* Incapable of violence  
* No offensive capabilities  
* No evidence of malicious intent  

The anomaly's primary effect is considered inconvenient rather than dangerous.  

Several researchers have argued that the effect is "mildly deserved."  

These comments have been removed from official documentation.
