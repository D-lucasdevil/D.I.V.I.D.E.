# File: LD-074 — "Judgement"   

**Designation:** DIVIDE Anomalous Weapon Platform   

**Threat Level:** Class III   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+  

**Containment Status:** Armed Containment Vault    








---  

## Overview  

LD-074, designated **"Judgement,"** is a hybrid anomalous weapon developed by DIVIDE's Advanced Weapons Division following the rise in Class III and Class IV anomaly encounters.  

The weapon combines:  

* High-caliber rifle  
* Monomolecular combat blade  

into a single platform.  

The weapon stands approximately 1.2 meters in length and weighs 94 kilograms.  

Despite its weight, authorized users report that it feels significantly lighter while wielded.   

Unauthorized personnel attempting to lift LD-074 experience rapid muscular failure and severe joint damage.  

---  

## Physical Description   

LD-074 consists of:   

* Reinforced tungsten-steel frame  
* Anomalous reactor core   
* Adaptive ammunition chamber   
* Integrated combat blade    

The blade extends nearly one meter from the weapon's front assembly.   

Material analysis has repeatedly failed.   

Samples removed from the blade disappear within seconds.   

---   

## Origin  

LD-074 was created during **Project JUDGEMENT**, a classified initiative intended to develop weapons capable of engaging powerful anomalies without requiring APA deployment.   

Development took seven years.   

Thirty-four prototype weapons failed.  

Only one successful platform was produced.   

The lead researcher disappeared three days after activation.   

His final note simply read:   

> "It works. It knows who deserves it."   

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### Adaptive Ammunition   

LD-074 generates its own ammunition.   

The weapon does not require reloading.  

Generated rounds adapt to the target being engaged.   

Observed effects include:   

* Armor penetration  
* Thermal impact   
* Electromagnetic disruption   
* Biological destabilization  

The weapon appears capable of determining which projectile is most effective against a target.   

---  

### Execution Mode   

When confronting particularly dangerous anomalies, LD-074 may enter what researchers call:   

## Execution Mode   

During activation:   

* Red energy illuminates the weapon.  
* Internal temperatures exceed safe limits.   
* The blade begins emitting red light.   

The next attack gains significantly increased effectiveness.   

However:   

The user suffers severe physical strain.   

Repeated use has resulted in:   

* Organ damage   
* Internal bleeding   
* Nervous system failure   

---  

### Blade Function  

The integrated blade can cut through:  

* Reinforced steel   
* Concrete   
* Armored vehicles   

Against anomalies, the blade demonstrates unusual effectiveness.   

Researchers believe the weapon partially ignores certain anomalous durability effects.   

---   

### Threat Recognition   

LD-074 appears partially sentient.   

The weapon continuously evaluates nearby individuals.   

Personnel carrying hostile intent toward DIVIDE report:   

* Feelings of dread   
* Hearing whispers   
* Sudden weapon malfunctions   

In one incident, LD-074 discharged without user input and severely injured a hostile infiltrator.   

No explanation has been found.   

---   

## Compatibility   

Only 11 individuals have successfully synchronized with LD-074.   

Others experience:   

* Seizures   
* Temporary blindness   
* Cardiac arrest  

One BERSERK operative described synchronization as:   

> "Like being judged by pure dominance."  

---  

## Incident Report 074-02  

A Class III containment breach occurred during transport.   

Three anomalies escaped simultaneously.   

LD-074 was issued to BERSERK Operative K-12.   

Security footage shows:   

* 47 shots fired   
* 12 blade strikes   
* Containment restored in 3 minutes 11 seconds   

The operative suffered six broken bones and complete muscular failure afterward.   

LD-074 showed no damage.   

---   

## Containment Procedures   

LD-074 is stored inside a dedicated weapons vault.   

Requirements:   
 
* Triple biometric authorization.   
* Continuous surveillance.   
* No ammunition storage required.   

If unauthorized access is attempted:   

Automated lockdown procedures activate immediately.   

---   

## Threat Assessment   

LD-074 is classified:  

### Class III   

Reasoning:   

* Highly effective against anomalies.   
* Semi-sentient behavior.   
* Significant combat potential.   

However:  

* Requires a compatible user.  
* Cannot operate independently.   
* Poses no large-scale extinction threat.
