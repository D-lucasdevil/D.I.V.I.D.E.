**Overview:**  

LD-045, designated “DIVA,” is a highly aggressive humanoid entity exhibiting extreme combat capability, regenerative properties, and anomalous damage output.  

**The entity appears as a young female with:**  

Short hair  
A bandage covering her eyes, partially concealing the face  
Glowing blood-red eyes visible through the wrapping  
A white jacket marked with irregular stripe patterns  

LD-045 is equipped with an anomalous weapon and armor set resembling Justitia-class gear

**Behavior:**  

**LD-045 displays:**  

Extreme hostility toward living anomalies  
Selective aggression toward non-living anomalies  
No interest in communication or negotiation  
Persistent combat-driven behavior  

The entity actively seeks out and attempts to eliminate anomalous targets on sight.  

**Combat Capabilities:**  

LD-045 is considered one of the most dangerous humanoid combat entities recorded.  

Primary Ability — “Life Force Damage”  
Attacks bypass physical durability  
Directly damage the target’s life force  
Victims experience rapid biological degradation regardless of armor or resistance  
Combat Techniques:  
High-speed melee combat using Justitia-class weapon  
Multi-strike attacks:  
5-hit burst strike  
10-hit execution sequence  
Extreme reaction speed and spatial awareness  
Capable of detecting targets through walls and obstacles  
Special Ability — [REDACTED] (“Blind Bird Manifestation”)  

LD-045 can manifest a large, shadow-like avian silhouette.  

**Effect:**  

All individuals within ~50m radius are:  
Instantly immobilized  
Suspended mid-air  
Terminated via forced cervical break  

**Limitation:**  

Special Ability Ineffective against entities equal to or stronger than LD-045  
Regeneration:  
Near-instant healing of:  
Ballistic wounds  
Structural damage  
Damage appears to increase aggression levels  
**Incident Record:**  

**Initial containment resulted in catastrophic losses:**  

6 B.E.S.E.R.K. Operatives — KIA  
8,267 DIVIDE Personnel — KIA  

Engagement ended only after intervention by APA-01, supported by an APA unit.  

LD-045 was successfully restrained following direct suppression.  

**Containment Protocol:**  
LD-045 is to be kept under maximum physical restraint at all times  
Containment chamber reinforced beyond Class IV standards  
APA-01 is to remain stationed within rapid-response distance  
**Emergency Directive:**  

**In the event of:**  

Containment breach  
Loss of restraint  
Uncontrolled escalation   

APA-01 is authorized to terminate LD-045 immediately.
