# File: LD-062 — “The Hollow Farm”  

**Designation:** Territorial Judgment/Environmental Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** Class II  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 3+  
**Containment Status:** Restricted Quarantine Zone Active  


![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HJf48YPXUAQ4_vt?format=jpg&name=large)


---  

## Overview  

LD-062, designated **“The Hollow Farm,”** is a localized anomalous zone centered around an abandoned rural farmhouse located deep within a remote forest region.  

The anomaly consists of:  

* A deteriorating red farmhouse  
* Approximately 2.4 square kilometers of surrounding land  
* Numerous unstable black subterranean formations designated:  

  * **LD-062-A (“The Pits”)**  

Unlike most territorial anomalies, LD-062 does not attack indiscriminately.  

Instead, the anomaly appears to selectively target individuals based on unknown behavioral or psychological criteria.  

The farmhouse itself is designated:  

## LD-062-B  

---  

## Physical Description  

LD-062-B resembles a heavily weathered wooden farmhouse showing severe structural decay.  

Despite this, the structure displays several abnormal characteristics:  

* Interior lights activate during nighttime hours despite no electrical systems existing  
* Human silhouettes are frequently visible through windows  
* Movement can occasionally be seen on the rooftop and surrounding property  
* Footsteps and knocking sounds are commonly reported inside the structure  

Thermal imaging repeatedly detects multiple humanoid heat signatures within the building.  

However, all interior searches have revealed the structure to be completely empty.  

Damage inflicted upon the farmhouse slowly repairs itself over time.  

---  

## LD-062-A — “The Pits”  

The primary anomalous behavior of LD-062 manifests through unstable black sinkhole-like formations appearing throughout the surrounding land after sunset.  

The pits resemble:  

* Pools of moving darkness  
* Liquid-black soil depressions  
* Shadow-like openings beneath the ground  

The formations appear silently and vanish before sunrise.  

No measurable depth has ever been determined.  

Objects lowered into active pits have never been recovered.  

---  

## Primary Anomalous Properties  

### Selective Targeting  

LD-062-A demonstrates highly selective predatory behavior.  

Research strongly suggests the pits primarily target individuals who have:  

## Never intentionally killed another human being.  

Affected individuals report:  

* Sudden heaviness in their legs  
* Soil becoming unstable beneath them  
* Strong downward pulling sensations  

Within seconds, victims are dragged beneath the ground entirely.  

Most disappearances occur too rapidly for rescue attempts.  

No confirmed victims have ever returned.  

---  

### Avoidance Behavior  

Individuals considered highly dangerous or violent appear significantly less likely to be targeted.  

Documented immune subjects include:  

* Violent repeat offenders  
* Combat veterans  
* Certain APA operatives  
* Serial homicide suspects  
* Several hostile anomalies  

In many cases, the pits actively avoid forming near these individuals.  

Researchers theorize LD-062 evaluates subjects based on:  

* Prior acts of lethal violence  
* Combat capability  
* Psychological aggression levels  

The exact mechanism remains unknown.  

---  

### Protective Phenomenon  

Rarely, LD-062-A displays behavior interpreted as protective toward exceptionally dangerous individuals.  

Documented incidents include:  

* Pits opening beneath hostile entities attacking armed personnel  
* Aggressive wildlife being dragged underground  
* Terrain shifting to obstruct approaching threats  

One APA operative reported:  

> “The ground moved around me like it was trying to keep me alive.”  

This phenomenon has led some researchers to theorize LD-062 may perceive certain individuals as:  

* Predators rather than prey  
* “Accepted” within the territory  
* Part of the anomaly’s ecosystem  

---  

## Weakness  

LD-062 demonstrates reduced activity during:  

* Daylight hours  
* Dry environmental conditions  

The pits become significantly less stable under:  

* Intense fire exposure  
* Floodlighting  
* Continuous seismic vibration  

However, no known method has permanently neutralized the anomaly.  

---  

## Incident Record — Red Creek Event  

LD-062 was discovered following a series of disappearances near an isolated rural community.  

Initial responding authorities reported:  

* Black ground formations moving beneath fields  
* Multiple civilians disappearing into the soil  
* Human silhouettes watching from the farmhouse windows  

Confirmed losses included:  

* 23 civilians  
* 4 local law enforcement officers  

Interestingly, several armed criminal suspects hiding within the region were found completely unharmed.  

One individual claimed:  

> “The ground swallowed everyone except us.”  

---  

## Containment Protocols  

LD-062 is currently contained through:  

* Permanent quarantine fencing  
* Armed perimeter patrols  
* Remote thermal surveillance  
* Strict nighttime access prohibition  

Containment requirements:  

* No civilian entry after sunset  
* Minimum two-person patrol groups  
* Psychological screening for assigned personnel  
* Immediate evacuation during pit expansion events  

Emergency protocol:  

If pit formations spread beyond containment borders:  

* Regional lockdown procedures initiated  
* Controlled incendiary suppression permitted  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-062 is classified as:  

## **Class II**  

Reasoning:  

* Dangerous but localized anomaly  
* Selective targeting behavior  
* Limited expansion capability  
* Containment achievable through quarantine enforcement  

Despite the high lethality toward civilians, LD-062 lacks:  

* Large-scale destructive capability  
* Rapid territorial spread  
* advanced offensive intelligence  

Researchers note the anomaly’s greatest danger is its unpredictably selective behavior rather than direct physical force.
