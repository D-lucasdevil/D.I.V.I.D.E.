# File: LD-058 — “The Hunter Tribes” / “Οι Κυνηγοί”  

**Designation:** Tribal Humanoid Species Collective  
**Threat Level:** Class III (Varies by Tribe)  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Global Monitoring Active  

---  

## Overview  

LD-058, collectively designated **“The Hunter Tribes”** or **“Οι Κυνηγοί”** (“The Hunters”), is a widespread species of anomalous humanoids documented across multiple regions of Earth.  

The species possesses several shared biological traits:  

* Average height of approximately 3 meters  
* Green, blue, or teal-tinted skin pigmentation  
* Seven-fingered hands consisting of:  

  * Five primary fingers  
  * Two opposable thumbs  
* Human-level intelligence  
* Advanced tribal social structures  

The Hunters have been referenced in:  

* Ancient cave paintings  
* Religious carvings  
* Prehistoric war murals  
* Maritime folklore  

Despite their primitive appearance, many tribes demonstrate:  

* Advanced combat tactics  
* Organized societies  
* Knowledge of anomalous biology  
* Limited supernatural capabilities  

LD-058 is currently divided into four major documented tribal factions.  

---  

# Tribal Factions  

---  

# “The Mother”  

**Threat Classification:** Class III Regional Threat

“The Mother” is the largest and most aggressive known Hunter tribe.  

This faction primarily inhabits dense regions of southern Indonesia and surrounding island territories.  

Unlike standard Hunter pigmentation, members possess:  

* Pale blue skin  
* Darkened eyes  
* Heavy scarification markings  

The tribe operates under a strict matriarchal hierarchy.  

Behavioral patterns include:  

* Coordinated nocturnal hunting  
* Ambush tactics against humans and anomalies  
* Large-scale wildlife harvesting  

Members are rarely seen interacting socially outside of:  

* Ritual gatherings  
* Hunting formations  
* Territorial conflicts  

Estimated population:  

## ~500,000 members    

Weaponry includes:    

* Bone spears    
* Bladed weapons  
* Reinforced bows  
* Anomalous organic weapon components  

Several recovered weapons remain biologically unidentified.  

The Mother tribe is responsible for multiple disappearances across isolated coastal settlements.  

---  

# “The Daughter”  

**Threat Classification:**  Class II Threat  

“The Daughter” tribe closely resembles The Mother faction but displays notably less hostility.  

This tribe was most recently identified within remote sectors of southern California.  

Distinctive traits include:  

* Teal-colored skin  
* More cooperative internal social behavior  
* Lower aggression toward humans  

Unlike The Mother, members are frequently observed:  

* Walking openly through settlements  
* Participating in communal activity  
* Conducting organized meetings   

Estimated population:  

## ~450,000 members  

Equipment remains similar to The Mother tribe but appears more refined and less brutal in construction.  

Direct attacks against humans are uncommon unless:  

* Territory is violated  
* Resources are threatened  
* Tribal leadership is endangered  

DIVIDE researchers suspect a shared ancestral origin between The Mother and The Daughter factions.  

---  

# “The Shining Armour”  

**Threat Classification:**  Class III Threat  

“The Shining Armour” is considered the most technologically and anomalously advanced Hunter tribe currently known.  

The tribe exists in two major roaming groups operating across separate global regions.  

Combined estimated population:  

## ~400,000 members  

This faction is strongly associated with:  

* LD-042  

Members are consistently observed accompanying LD-042 during:  

* Feeding cycles  
* Rest periods  
* Underground migration events  

The tribe demonstrates no fear toward the anomaly and frequently climbs directly onto its body.  

Unlike other factions, The Shining Armour possesses:  

* Forged metal weaponry  
* Iron-tipped arrows  
* Reinforced armor  
* Anomalous energy manipulation capabilities  

Documented abilities include:  

* Energy projection  
* Lightning generation  
* Temporary defensive barrier creation  

Research suggests these abilities may originate from prolonged exposure to LD-042.  

The tribe remains mostly passive toward humans and anomalies unless anything approaches within:  

## 400 meters of LD-042  

At that point, members become immediately hostile and highly coordinated.  

---

# “The Fae Butcher”  

**Threat Classification:** Class II Threat  

“The Fae Butcher” is the smallest and most socially tolerant Hunter tribe documented.  

Estimated population:  

## ~200,000 members  

Unlike other factions, this tribe allows limited human presence within its territories.  

The tribe specializes in hunting:  

* Small fairy-like anomalous organisms  
* Primarily instances of LD-040 they have been seen traveling around asia and europe hunting fleeing LD-040s. 

Members utilize:  

* Fire-tipped arrows  
* Specialized steel weapons producing severe burns to LD-040 organisms  

The tribe appears largely responsible for the low population count of LD-040 across Europe and Asia.  

Harvested materials are used for:  

* Weapons  
* Cutlery  
* Medicinal compounds  
* Ritualistic tools  

The tribe actively breeds contained LD-040 specimens inside reinforced stone enclosures lined with specialized metal restraints.  

Medicinal substances derived from LD-040 remain toxic to humans but highly effective for Hunter physiology.  

Observed effects on Hunters include:  

* Accelerated healing  
* Increased stamina  
* Resistance to infection  

Due to extensive predation, LD-040 entities actively avoid regions populated by The Fae Butcher.  

---  

## Shared Species Behavior  

Despite tribal differences, all Hunter factions display:  

* Territorial instincts  
* Cooperative combat behavior  
* Complex language systems  
* Knowledge of anomaly ecosystems  

The species demonstrates an unusual understanding of anomalous entities far beyond expected tribal intelligence.  

Several researchers suspect LD-058 predates recorded human civilization.  

---  

## Containment Status  

LD-058 cannot currently be contained due to:  

* Massive population numbers  
* Global distribution  
* Environmental adaptability  
* High combat capability in several tribes  

DIVIDE instead maintains:  

* Surveillance operations  
* Territorial mapping  
* Limited diplomatic observation with select factions  

Direct extermination attempts have consistently failed.  

---

## Threat Assessment  

LD-058 is collectively classified as:  

## **Class III**  

Reasoning:  

* Extremely large population  
* Advanced combat coordination  
* Partial anomalous abilities  
* Potential alliances with other anomalies  

Threat levels vary significantly depending on:  

* Tribe encountered  
* Territorial conditions  
* Nearby anomaly activity  

While certain factions remain manageable, others possess enough strength to threaten regional stability directly.
