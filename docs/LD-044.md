***File: LD-044 — “The Veiled Judgment”***  

**Designation: Unknown Entity / Autonomous Execution Phenomenon**  
**Threat Level: [REDACTED]**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 5+**  
**Containment Status: Partially Contained / Unreliable**  



![LD-006 — Civilian Photo](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HEsH8X3XoAA4PJq?format=jpg&name=large)




**Overview:**  

LD-044 is a humanoid entity resembling a female nun, dressed in black and red ceremonial clothing. The entity’s eyes are permanently covered by a luxurious, aged bandage, tightly wrapped and stained. It wears white gloves and maintains a composed, upright posture at all times.  

**The entity is completely non-responsive to:**  

Verbal communication  
Environmental changes  

Despite this, LD-044 is classified as cooperative, as it does not display aggression unless specific unknown conditions are met.  

**Behavioral Pattern:**  

At unpredictable intervals, LD-044 will:  

Become motionless for several seconds  
Slowly raise one arm  
Point directly at a specific individual  

**Upon being targeted, the victim experiences immediate and catastrophic internal failure:**  

Internal organs violently rupture and invert  
Flesh tears outward from within  
Thousands of cruciform lacerations appear across the body  
Death occurs within seconds  

**After the event:**  

LD-044 clasps its hands  
Audibly whispers:  

“Mors est verus deus.”  

It then returns to a passive state.  

**Targeting Anomaly:**  

Certain Death.  

**Confirmed observations:**  

Targets include:  
Criminals  
Military personnel  
Civilians  
Children  
Individuals with no recorded wrongdoing  
Religious affiliation does not provide immunity  
Moral alignment appears irrelevant or inconsistent  
Containment Failure Events:  

**LD-044 has repeatedly:**  

Disappeared from containment  
Reappeared without warning  
Been linked to global incidents matching its execution pattern  

Recovered bodies from these events display identical characteristics to in-facility terminations.  

Duration of disappearance:  

Ranges from minutes to several days  

Tracking attempts have failed completely.  

**Containment Attempts:**  
Standard containment chamber: ineffective  
Restraints: ignored / bypassed  
Surveillance: intermittent failure during disappearance events  
Incident Log 044-B:  

An AT-Class subject was instructed to fire upon LD-044.  

**Result:**  

Firearm malfunctioned mid-action (round halted inside chamber)  
LD-044 immediately targeted the subject  
Subject terminated via standard internal rupture event  

No further hostile testing authorized.  

**Limitations:**  

Unknown.  

**LD-044 has shown:**  

No measurable durability limits  
No reaction time delay  
No resistance threshold  

All attempts to define its boundaries have resulted in loss of personnel.  

**Current Assessment:**  

LD-044 is considered:  

Unpredictable  
Uncontainable under current methods  
Potentially operating under external or non-human judgment criteria  

Threat Level remains [REDACTED] pending further understanding.  

**Internal Memorandum:**  

“It doesn’t punish evil.  
It doesn’t spare good.  
It just… chooses.”  

— DIVIDE Oversight
