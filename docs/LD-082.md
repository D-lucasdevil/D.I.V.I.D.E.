# File: LD-082 — "The Mist Hound"     

**Designation:** Territorial Bestial Entity   

**Threat Level:** Class IV     

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+    

**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Exclusion Zone Established     

---  

## Overview   

LD-082, designated **"The Mist Warden,"** is an enormous quadrupedal anomaly resembling a grotesquely malformed wolf composed of countless limbs, elongated vertebrae, and dozens of canine heads. The creature appears permanently surrounded by a dense supernatural fog with a radius varying between **3 and 8 kilometers**, making aerial observation and satellite tracking impossible.    

Unlike most Class IV anomalies, LD-082 has **never left its territory**. Every recorded attack has occurred only after an individual entered the fog.    

The purpose of its territorial behavior remains unknown.   

---   

## Physical Description    

LD-082 measures approximately **32 meters** in total length and nearly **11 meters** at shoulder height.   

Its anatomy appears biologically impossible.    

Observed features include:    

* One primary body.   
* Between **70–150 functioning legs**, the exact number constantly changing.    
* Dozens of partially formed forelimbs protruding from the torso.    
* Numerous canine heads capable of operating independently.    
* Exposed muscle fibers that continuously regenerate.    
* A tail nearly fifteen meters long lined with grasping limbs.     

Researchers have noted that the total number of heads fluctuates constantly.    

Some simply grow.    

Others fall away and rot into black sludge before being replaced within minutes.    

---     

## Discovery    

LD-082 first appeared following a **Class IV Pulse Event** in northern Europe.   

Within forty-eight hours, an entire mountain valley disappeared beneath an unnatural fog bank.   

Three villages totaling **1,148 residents** vanished shortly afterward.    

Recovery teams discovered only abandoned vehicles, weapons, and equipment.   

No bodies were ever recovered.    

The fog has remained in place ever since.    

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### Endless Heads   

The creature continuously generates new heads.   

Destroyed heads regenerate within seconds to minutes depending on the extent of damage.    

Removing heads appears only to slow the anomaly temporarily.     

Researchers estimate its regenerative capacity is fueled by an unknown internal organ that has never been observed.    

---    

### Extreme Regeneration   

Nearly every wound closes within moments.   
 
Lost limbs regenerate.    
 
Destroyed muscle reforms.    
 
Missing heads regrow.    

However, unlike truly immortal anomalies, sufficient overwhelming damage does permanently destroy tissue faster than it can regenerate.     

---   

### Living Fog    

The surrounding mist is itself anomalous.    

Inside the fog:    

* Visibility rarely exceeds five meters.    
* GPS becomes unusable.    
* Radio communication deteriorates rapidly.    
* Personnel frequently report hearing barking, crying children, or distant voices.    

No hallucinations have been conclusively confirmed.    

---    
 
### Territorial Instinct     

LD-082 never pursues prey outside the fog.     

Survivors who manage to escape report the creature immediately halting pursuit once the fog boundary is crossed.     

Because of this behavior, researchers believe it is guarding something rather than hunting indiscriminately.     

---   

## Combat Capability   

When engaging targets, LD-082 displays terrifying aggression.    

Observed attacks include:   

* Crushing armored vehicles beneath its mass.    
* Using dozens of limbs to pin victims.    
* Coordinated bites from multiple heads simultaneously.     
* Charging at speeds exceeding 90 km/h despite its enormous size.     

It demonstrates remarkable battlefield awareness, often sacrificing expendable heads to expose enemies before attacking with its primary body.    

---    

## Containment Attempts   

Five containment operations have been authorized.    

All have failed.    

Artillery bombardment temporarily incapacitates the anomaly, but once shelling ceases it begins regenerating.     

During **Operation Grey Forest**, sustained bombardment lasting twenty-seven minutes removed nearly eighty percent of the creature's visible mass.     

Recovery drones sent thirty minutes later recorded LD-082 standing once again with no visible injuries.    

---    

## Weaknesses   

Despite its immense regenerative ability, LD-082 is **not immortal**.     

Theoretical simulations conclude the anomaly could be terminated through:     

* Sustained heavy artillery.    
* Strategic bunker-penetrating munitions.    
* Simultaneous destruction of its entire central body.    

Unfortunately, accomplishing this within the fog has proven operationally impossible.     

---    

## The Protected Area    

Ground-penetrating radar detects an unidentified structure approximately **600 meters** beyond the center of LD-082's territory.    

Every attempt to approach this location has resulted in complete loss of personnel.    

No visual confirmation has ever been obtained.   

Current leading theories suggest LD-082 is guarding:   

* An undiscovered Pulse Core.    
* A dormant Class V or higher anomaly.     
* The remains of whatever created it.     

No theory has been confirmed.    

---    

## Threat Assessment    

LD-082 is classified **Class IV** due to:    

* Exceptional regenerative capabilities.   
* Massive physical strength.   
* Near-impenetrable territorial defense.   
* The strategic danger posed by its anomalous fog.   

The creature's refusal to leave its territory is the primary reason it has not received a higher classification.
