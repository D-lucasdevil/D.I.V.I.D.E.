# **File: LD-053 — “The Crimson Impaler”**  

**Designation:** Humanoid Warfare Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** **Class IV**  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 5+  
**Containment Status:** Temporary Containment Achieved — Maximum Surveillance Active  

---  

# **Overview**  

LD-053, designated **“The Crimson Impaler,”** is a highly intelligent humanoid combat anomaly specializing in large-scale impalement, blood manipulation, and adaptive battlefield escalation.  

The entity resembles a towering armored figure draped in crimson ceremonial cloth concealing its face entirely. Beneath the veil, no confirmed biological structure has been observed.  

LD-053 carries an anomalous spear of constantly shifting structure:  

* Length fluctuates during combat  
* Material composition unknown  
* Edge capable of piercing:  

  * Reinforced steel  
  * Heavy armor plating  
  * Concrete fortifications  

The weapon appears partially organic during periods of heightened aggression.  

---  

# **Behavior**  

Unlike most aggressive anomalies, LD-053 demonstrates:  

* Tactical intelligence  
* Battlefield awareness  
* Emotional restraint  

The entity:  

* Walks calmly during engagements  
* Rarely speaks  
* Displays ceremonial behavior toward impaled victims  

Bodies killed by LD-053 are frequently:  

* Elevated on spears  
* Positioned publicly  
* Arranged deliberately  

Research suggests:  

> The anomaly gains strength from active impalement displays.  

---  

# **Primary Abilities**  

## **Spear Manifestation**  

LD-053 can generate:  

> An effectively infinite number of spears  

Manifestation locations include:  

* Ground surfaces  
* Walls  
* Airspace  
* Beneath targets  

Generated spears vary in:  

* Shape  
* Length  
* Structure  

---  

# **Blood Extraction**  

LD-053 can forcibly extract blood from living targets within close proximity.  

Observed effects:  

* Rapid exsanguination  
* Organ rupture  
* Instant physical weakening  

Extracted blood is absorbed directly into the anomaly’s body, accelerating:  

* Regeneration  
* Physical strength  
* Reaction speed  

---  

# **Escalation State System**  

LD-053 becomes progressively stronger depending on:  

> Number of active impaled victims remaining displayed.  

The anomaly’s combat capabilities scale dramatically with battlefield casualties.  

---  

## **10 Impalements Reached**  

Abilities unlocked:  

* 500-meter area impalement pulse  
* Simultaneous spear eruptions across nearby terrain  

At this stage:  

> Full BERSERK deployment becomes mandatory.  

---  

## **10,000 Impalements Reached**  

Abilities unlocked:  

* Long-range spear manifestation  
* Atmospheric spear strikes from above  
* Instantaneous subterranean impalement attacks  

At this level, LD-053 approaches:  

> Large-scale extinction threat potential.  

---  

# **Weakness**  

LD-053’s power source appears linked directly to:  

> Existing impaled corpses.  

Destroying or removing displayed victims:  

* Weakens the anomaly significantly  
* Reduces regeneration  
* Limits large-scale abilities  

This weakness was critical during its first successful containment.

Initial Pulse event caused immediate mass casualty escalation.  

Confirmed losses:  

* 27,000 conventional military personnel

* Massive impalement fields

During the final containment phase:  

BERSERK Squad initiated:  

* High-speed corpse destruction operations

> APA Squad arrival.

* A vertically reinforced anti-weapon containment structure  

Containment requirements:  

* Zero organic personnel within direct chamber radius  
* No exposed corpses permitted nearby  
* Automated disposal systems active at all times  
 
Emergency protocol:  
If active impalement count exceeds:  

> 10 confirmed victims  

Then:  

* Full BERSERK mobilization authorized  
* APA Squad deployment immediate
