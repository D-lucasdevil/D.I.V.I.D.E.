# Black Streets

**Designation: Illicit Trade Zone / Anomalous Market Hub**  
**Threat Level: Class III (Localized)**  
**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 1+**  
**Containment Status: Monitored / Indirectly Controlled**  

**Overview:**  

Black Street is a highly secured, underground trade zone located within the Czech Republic at [REDACTED]. It functions as a global marketplace for illegal, restricted, and anomalous goods, operating beyond conventional law enforcement and partially tolerated by DIVIDE.  

The location is considered the second most protected area in the world, surpassed only by DIVIDE central command infrastructure.  

Black Street is widely known among criminal networks, private collectors, rogue researchers, and certain government contacts. Despite its illegal nature, it operates under strict internal order and enforcement.  

**Trade & Economy:**  

**Black Street allows the buying and selling of:**  

Anomalies (primary commodity)  
Military-grade weapons (including restricted-class armaments)  
Controlled substances  
Human organs (with on-site surgical staff)  
Classified documents and technology  
Anomaly Market Value (Estimated):  
Class I: $20M – $50M  
Class II: $100M – $500M  
Class III: Rare — up to $2B+  

**Item-type anomalies are significantly more valuable than living entities due to:**  

Stability  
Ease of transport  
Controllability  

**Living anomalies are rarely sold unless:**  

Deceased  
Heavily contained  
Or deemed low-risk  
DIVIDE Involvement:  

Despite its nature, DIVIDE does not shut down Black Street.  

**Reasoning:**  

Serves as a centralized anomaly funnel  
Allows tracking of high-risk artifacts  
Provides opportunity to purchase or confiscate anomalies  

**DIVIDE protocol:**  

**Any discovered anomaly can be:**  
Purchased  
Temporarily confiscated for analysis  
Permanently seized if deemed dangerous but they will be offered the average sum of money that tier of abnormality sells for 
If an individual resists and weaponizes an anomaly:  
Immediate termination authorized  
Artifact recovered without negotiation  
Nuclear weapon transactions are strictly monitored  
Unauthorized buyers are executed without warning  

**Black Street enforces absolute internal law**  

**The following are strictly forbidden:**  

Murder  
Assault  
Sexual violence  
Theft  

**Punishment:**  

Immediate execution  
Public skinning of the offender  

These punishments are carried out publicly to maintain order and deter disruption.  

**Security & Protection:**  

Black Street is guarded by high-level enforcement entities, referred to as “Protectors.”  

Standard Protectors: Comparable to Class III anomalies  
Elite Protectors: Comparable to low Class IV anomalies  

**Their presence ensures:**  

Zero conflict tolerance  
Total control of all activity  
Rapid neutralization of threats

**Residency:**  

**Permanent residence within Black Street is possible but:**

Extremely expensive  
Restricted to high-value individuals  
Constantly monitored  

**Residents include:**   

Black market traders  
Rogue scientists  
Wealthy anomaly collectors  
Notable Transaction:  
LD-016 sold for $220,000,000 USD  
Included additional agreement: DIVIDE protection contract  


**Risk Assessment:**  

**While dangerous, Black Street is considered:**

More stable than decentralized black markets  
Easier to monitor than global anomaly smuggling  
A necessary evil within the current system  
**Internal Note:**  

“You don’t shut down Black Street.
You watch it… and make sure the wrong people don’t walk out with the wrong things.”  

— DIVIDE Oversight Command  

**Classification Notice:**  

This file is accessible to Level 1 personnel due to the widespread awareness of Black Street within both civilian and underground networks.
