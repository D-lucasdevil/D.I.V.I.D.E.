# File: LD-076 — "The Silent Queen"  

**Designation:** Sapient Humanoid / Plague Entity  

**Threat Level:** Class IV  

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+  

**Containment Status:** Special Containment Active   

---   

## Overview   

LD-076, designated **"The Silent Queen,"** is a humanoid female anomaly measuring approximately 1.8 meters in height. The entity is permanently covered in a black substance resembling liquid tar, hardened bone, and metallic    growths. Large sword-like protrusions emerge from her armor-like exterior and continuously grow before eventually breaking away.   

The anomaly has never spoken, communicated, or displayed any recognizable emotional response despite extensive observation.   

LD-076 is responsible for the manifestation of an anomalous infectious substance designated **Black Plaque**.   

The entity's arrival was accompanied by a confirmed **Class IV Pulse Event**.    

---   


LD-076 appears as a heavily armored female figure composed of:   

* Black tar-like biological material.   
* Metallic skeletal growths.   
* Self-generating sword structures.   
* White skin visible through cracks in the armor.   

Analysis indicates the "armor" is part of the entity's body.   

Attempts to remove samples result in rapid decay of recovered material.   

---   

## Black Plaque   

The primary anomalous effect associated with LD-076 is an infectious substance known as **Black Plaque**.    

The substance spreads through:   

* Physical contact.   
* Bodily fluids.   
* Direct exposure to contaminated structures.   

Infected areas gradually develop:   

* Black organic growths.   
* Metallic sword-like formations.   
* Hardened plaque deposits.   

The spread is relatively slow compared to most biological outbreaks but becomes increasingly difficult to stop once established.   

---   

## Servant Creation   

Individuals reaching advanced stages of infection undergo complete bodily restructuring.   
 
Observed changes include:   

* Skeletal distortion.   
* Growth of blade-like appendages.   
* Severe tissue replacement.   
* Complete loss of individual identity.   

These entities are designated **Plaque Servants**.   

Plaque Servants display unquestioning loyalty toward LD-076.   

The Silent Queen has been observed directing large groups of Servants through simple gestures despite never speaking.   

---   

## Behavior   

LD-076 is extremely hostile toward intrusion.   

Any unauthorized entry into her containment zone results in one of two outcomes:   

1. Immediate attack.   
2. Forced infection.   

Researchers have been unable to determine how the entity selects between these responses.   

The anomaly refuses all attempts at:   

* Communication.   
* Negotiation.   
* Testing.   
* Observation at close range.   

To date, not a single word has been recorded from the entity.   

---  

## Containment   

LD-076 is housed within a dedicated containment sector constructed around the infected region itself.   

Due to extreme contamination:   

* No personnel are permitted inside the primary chamber.   
* Observation occurs remotely.   
* Automated systems perform maintenance.   
* Emergency sterilization measures remain active at all times.   

Approximately 93% of the containment chamber is currently covered by Black Plaque growth.   

---   

## Incident 076-01   

The arrival of LD-076 remains one of the most destructive Class IV emergence events on record.   

Upon manifestation, containment forces suffered catastrophic casualties attempting to halt the spread.   

The situation escalated until **LD-050** breached containment without authorization.   

Surveillance footage shows LD-050 immediately identifying LD-076 as a threat and engaging.   

The resulting battle lasted approximately 17 minutes.   

During the final phase of combat, LD-050 entered an unprecedented state of physical deterioration later designated:   

### "Death's Door Mode"   

Despite suffering extreme injuries, LD-050 forcibly tore open sections of her chest armor and released a concentrated beam of plasma and thermal energy.    

The attack:   

* Vaporized thousands of tons of Black Plaque.   
* Destroyed countless Servants.    
* Severely damaged LD-076.   
* Prevented a regional outbreak.    

Following the attack, LD-050 removed LD-076's head from her body and reportedly shouted:   

> "I'M GONNA DIE, BUT I WIN! DO YOU UNDERSTAND?!"   

LD-076 offered no response.   

The entity regenerated several hours later after containment teams recovered the head.    

---      

## Threat Assessment   

LD-076 is classified **Class IV** due to:  

* Large-scale infection capability.   
* Ability to create loyal Servant entities.   
* Extreme regenerative properties.   
* Failure of conventional containment methods.   
* Direct survival against LD-050.
