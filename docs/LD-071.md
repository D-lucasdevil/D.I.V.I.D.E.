# File: LD-071 — "Killstreak"   

**Designation:** Anomalous Tool  

**Threat Level:** Class III  

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+  

**Containment Status:** Heavy Containment — Dedicated Blacksite Facility  

---  

## Overview  

LD-071, designated **"Killstreak,"** is a metallic crimson glove of unknown origin.  

The glove is constructed from an unidentified red-black alloy and displays several anomalous features:  

* A floating crimson orb constantly orbiting the wrist.  
* A permanent grin-like opening across the palm.  
* Dark spiral markings covering the entire surface.  
* Constant surface temperature of 37°C regardless of environment.  

When worn, a glowing red number appears above the user's head.  

The number represents the current **Kill Count** accumulated by the glove.  

---  

## Activation  

LD-071 immediately bonds to the wearer upon physical contact.  

Removal is impossible until:  

* User death.  
* Glove deactivation.  
* Authorized anomaly removal procedures.  

The glove provides minimal physical enhancement on its own.  

Its primary ability activates through successful kills.  

---  

# Kill Count System  

Each confirmed kill increases the visible number by one.  

Only sapient organisms count toward progression.  

Animals, insects, corpses, and most anomalies do not contribute.  

Researchers believe the glove specifically seeks:  

> "Intentional victories."  

---  

## Phase One — 5 Kills  

Effects:  

* Increased striking force.  
* Enhanced reaction speed.  
* Reduced fatigue.  

Threat increase remains minimal.  

---  

## Phase Two — 10 Kills  

Effects:  

* User can release short-range kinetic bursts through physical contact.  
* Bone fractures become common in unarmored targets.  

---  

## Phase Three — 25 Kills  

Effects:  

* Palm grin opens.  
* Successful strikes may cause severe internal trauma.  

Instant death is possible against ordinary humans.  

High durability targets remain largely unaffected.  

---  

## Phase Four — 50 Kills  

Effects:  

* Temporary regeneration.  
* Increased durability.  
* Enhanced movement speed.  

At this stage the user becomes a major threat to standard personnel.  

---  

## Phase Five — 75 Kills  

Effects:  

* Kinetic bursts become stronger.  
* User can briefly ignore severe injuries.  

---  

## Phase Six — 100 Kills  

Effects:  

* Peak recorded state.  

User gains:  

* Significant physical enhancement.  
* High durability.  
* Accelerated healing.  

---  

## Hard Limit  

Despite popular rumors, LD-071 does not scale infinitely.  

Testing indicates:  

## 100 Kills is the maximum effective threshold.  

Any kills beyond 100 provide no further enhancement.  

The visible counter continues increasing, but power output remains unchanged.  

This limitation is believed to be an intentional design feature.  

---  

## Interaction With Anomalies  

LD-071 demonstrates poor effectiveness against high-tier anomalies.  

Testing confirms:  

* Class I anomalies can be killed normally.  
* Class II anomalies suffer significant damage.  
* Class III anomalies are resistant.  
* Class IV anomalies appear completely immune.  



---  

## Special Interaction — LD-047   

During simulations, LD-047 displayed an unusual fixation on LD-071.  

When shown images of the glove, she repeatedly stated:  

> "One thousand."  

Researchers observed:  

* Increased aggression.    
* Obsessive behavior.   
* Attempts to acquire the artifact.    

However, projections and tests indicate that even if LD-047 obtained LD-071:   

* The glove would not elevate her beyond Class IV.  
* Power growth would still stop at 100 kills.   
* Higher-tier anomalies would remain resistant.    

The primary concern is not raw power.   

The concern is that LD-047 would actively attempt to reach maximum charge as quickly as possible.    

---   

## Discovery    

LD-071 was discovered during a BERSERK reconnaissance operation inside a remote forest.   

Personnel encountered:   

* Thousands of skulls.   
* Large quantities of dried blood.    
* A crimson glow visible from over 800 meters.    

The glove was located atop the skull mound.    

Oddly, none of the surrounding deaths contributed to the kill counter.    

The number displayed:    

> 5    

It remains at 5 to this day.   

Researchers remain uncertain why the previous victims were rejected.    

---    

## Threat Assessment  

LD-071 is classified:    

### Class III   

Reasoning:    

* Dangerous scaling potential.   
* High lethality against humans.   
* Strong combat utility.    

However:   

* Possesses a hard power ceiling.   
* Requires direct user involvement.   
* Ineffective against major anomalies.    
* Cannot independently cause extinction-level events.
