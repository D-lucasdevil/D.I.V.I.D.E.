# File: LD-078 — "The Golden-Eyed Lord"   

**Designation:** Humanoid Royal Entity    

**Threat Level:** Class IV   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 6+   

**Containment Status:** Hostile Containment Active   

---   

## Overview   

LD-078, designated **"The Golden-Eyed Lord"** is a humanoid male anomaly believed to be the former ruler of an unknown kingdom lost to history.  

The entity possesses long crimson hair, pale skin heavily damaged by continuous self-inflicted deterioration, and two luminous golden eyes that serve as the source of many of his anomalous abilities.   

LD-078 is one of the most dangerous sapient entities currently contained by DIVIDE.   

Despite possessing the intelligence and speech capabilities of a highly educated human, the anomaly remains almost completely uncooperative and displays extreme hostility toward containment personnel.    

The only exception to this behavior appears to be **LD-077 ("The White Maiden")**, toward whom LD-078 exhibits obsessive attachment.   

---   

## Physical Description   

LD-078 appears as a male approximately 2.1 meters tall.   

Its body displays continuous tissue damage despite remaining fully functional.   

Researchers have observed:   

* Flesh constantly burning away.   
* Continuous regeneration.   
* Golden light escaping wounds.   
* Internal temperatures exceeding survivable limits.   

The anomaly appears to exist in a permanent state of destruction and restoration.   

---   

## Historical Records   

Recovered texts from the pulse suggest LD-078 was once a prince who murdered his father to seize a royal throne.   

Following his ascension, the kingdom experienced a period now designated:  

### The Golden Purge   

During this period:   

* Entire cities vanished.  
* Royal armies disappeared.   
* Population records collapsed.   

The few surviving accounts claim the king personally slaughtered tens of thousands.   

Following these events, LD-078 reportedly acquired his anomalous abilities.   

The exact mechanism remains unknown.   

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### Eyes of Dominion   

LD-078's golden eyes serve as living anomalous organs.   

The entity is capable of removing either eye without injury.   

Once removed, the eye can be crushed in the hand and transformed into an anomalous weapon.   

Observed manifestations include:   

* Spears  
* Scythes  
* Greatswords   
* Halberds   
* Lances   

These weapons possess durability exceeding most conventional materials.   

Destroyed weapons eventually revert into golden particles before reforming as regenerated eyes.   

New eyes regrow within seconds.   

---   

### Blood Armaments   

LD-078 can also weaponize its own blood.   

Unlike Eye Weapons, Blood Weapons are:   
 
* Less durable   
* Less powerful   
* Faster to create   

These constructs are typically used during large-scale combat.   

---   

### Crownlight Corrosion   

A constant field of golden energy surrounds LD-078.   

Personnel entering this field experience:   

* Severe burns   
* Tissue decay   
* Cellular breakdown   
* Organ failure   

The effect intensifies as distance decreases.   

Standard protective equipment provides little resistance.   

The phenomenon is believed responsible for over 70% of containment casualties.   

---   

### Royal Authority   

When enraged, LD-078 can temporarily manifest hundreds of floating golden weapons simultaneously.   

These weapons can:   

* Attack independently   
* Pursue targets   
* Form defensive barriers   

Researchers estimate the anomaly intentionally limits its use of this ability.   

---   

### Regenerative Immortality   

LD-078 displays exceptional regeneration.   

The anomaly has survived:   

* Decapitation   
* Dismemberment   
* Incineration   
* High-yield explosives   

Containment teams report that severe damage only slows the entity.   

Permanent destruction has not been achieved.   

---   

## Relationship With LD-077   

The relationship between LD-077 and LD-078 remains one of DIVIDE's greatest mysteries.   

LD-078 frequently demands access to LD-077.   

LD-077 consistently refuses all contact.   

When questioned regarding the anomaly, LD-077 stated:   

> "I cannot follow him upon the path he chose."   

No additional explanation has been provided.   

---   

## Incident 078-07   

A researcher proposed testing whether LD-077's defensive barrier would react to LD-078.   

The proposal was immediately rejected.   

Merely informing LD-078 that LD-077 was nearby triggered the most violent containment breach in the entity's recorded history.   

Containment Block Gamma was destroyed.   

Seven kilometers of facility infrastructure suffered catastrophic damage.   

The anomaly repeatedly shouted:   

> "Where is she?"   

before being subdued.   

---   

## Containment Procedures   

LD-078 is contained within a heavily reinforced subterranean chamber.   

The chamber is subjected to:   

* Continuous radiation exposure.   
* Extreme thermal bombardment.   
* Automated weapon suppression systems.   
 
These measures do not injure the anomaly permanently.   

However, they significantly slow regeneration and reduce the effectiveness of Crownlight Corrosion.   

The anomaly remains conscious at all times.    

---   

## Threat Assessment   

LD-078 is classified:   

### Class IV   

Due to:   

* Large-scale destructive capabilities.   
* Near-immortality.   
* Extreme hostility.   
* Weapon manifestation abilities.   
* Potential interaction risks involving LD-077.
