# File: LD-084 — "The Mourning Coffin"   

**Designation:** Symbiotic Anomalous Entity / Living Sarcophagus   

**Threat Level:** Class II   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+   

**Containment Status:** Passive Containment — Stable   






---   

## Overview    

LD-084, designated **"The Mourning Coffin,"** consists of two inseparable anomalous components:    

* **LD-084-A:** A pale female humanoid permanently fused to an ornate living coffin.     
* **LD-084-B:** The coffin itself, an intelligent anomalous construct capable of independent movement and reality-altering effects within its interior.    

Unlike most Class II anomalies, LD-084 displays remarkably little hostility. She spends nearly all observed time peacefully resting inside the coffin or slowly wandering within her containment chamber.    

Personnel frequently describe her as appearing exhausted rather than dangerous.    

However, attempts to disturb the coffin have repeatedly demonstrated why the anomaly remains under permanent containment.    

---    

## Discovery     

LD-084 was discovered inside the ruins of an abandoned cathedral after reports that people entering its crypt simply "never screamed."    

Investigation teams located a single crimson coffin surrounded by thousands of human skulls.     

Inside rested a young woman peacefully asleep.     

When researchers attempted to remove the coffin, the chamber doors sealed by unknown means before every team member simultaneously reported overwhelming feelings of regret.     

Only one researcher survived.     

His only statement was:     

> "She asked if I wanted to rest."      

He accepted.     

He never woke again.     

---     

# Physical Description    

LD-084-A appears as a pale woman estimated to be approximately twenty years old.    

Characteristics include:    

* Extremely pale skin.    
* Long silver-white hair.    
* White ceremonial gown permanently attached to her lower body.    
* Slow, graceful movement.    
* Almost constant expression of sadness.    

Medical imaging suggests her spine, ribs, and lower organs have completely merged with the rear interior wall of the coffin.   

Despite this, she experiences no observable pain.    

The coffin itself appears grown rather than constructed.    

Bone, wood, stone and unknown organic tissue seamlessly merge into a single structure.    

---    

# Passive Behavior    

LD-084 rarely acknowledges personnel.    

Instead she may:    

* Read old books.    
* Sit silently beside her coffin.    
* Rearrange flowers placed inside her chamber.     
* Sleep for weeks without movement.    

She has never requested food or water.    

---    

# The Coffin     

LD-084-B is believed to possess limited intelligence independent of its owner.    

It responds differently depending on who attempts to enter.    

---    

## Gentle Passage    

If LD-084 personally invites an individual into the coffin...    

She carefully helps them lie down before quietly closing the lid.       

The subject experiences:     

* Complete absence of fear.     
* Warmth.    
* Memories of loved ones.     
* A painless loss of consciousness.      

Death occurs peacefully several minutes later.     

Autopsies reveal no trauma whatsoever.      

Cause of death remains unknown.     

Personnel have nicknamed this phenomenon:     

**"The Beautiful Death."**    

---

## Judgment of Regret    

If an individual:     

* Attempts to enter without permission...    
* Damages the coffin...    
* Or is judged by the anomaly to have lived a life consumed by regret, cruelty or wasted potential...     

The coffin's interior changes dramatically.     

Survivors do not exist.      

Internal cameras have never successfully recorded the phenomenon.     

Recovered remains display anatomical distortions impossible by known biological means.     

Researchers unanimously agree the interior dimensions of the coffin vastly exceed its external size.     

---    

# Skull Manifestation    

Although normally passive, LD-084 can summon enormous quantities of human skulls seemingly from nowhere.    

These skulls possess anomalous durability and obey simple telekinetic commands.     

Observed uses include:     

* Defensive barriers.    
* Crushing attacks.     
* High-speed projectiles.     
* Massive tidal waves of skeletal remains.     

During Containment Incident 084-03, enough skulls manifested to completely fill the containment chamber.     

Pressure from the accumulation caused reinforced concrete walls to crack before emergency suppression measures restored containment.     

Throughout the entire event...       

LD-084 remained peacefully asleep inside the coffin.      

---     

# Defensive Response     

LD-084 almost never initiates violence.     

However, if either she or her coffin is deliberately threatened...     

Her demeanor changes instantly.     

The chamber temperature drops.      

The skulls begin emerging.      

The coffin opens.     

Researchers estimate less than ten seconds remain before complete skeletal manifestation begins.     

Fortunately, every recorded incident ended after personnel retreated.     

---     

# Intelligence      

LD-084 is capable of speech.      

She rarely says more than a few sentences.     

Documented examples include:    

> "Everyone deserves rest."     

> "Some people simply arrive too late."    

> "You are not ready."      

She refuses to explain how the coffin judges individuals.    

---    

# Psychological Effects     

Personnel assigned longer than three months frequently report:     

* Reduced fear of death.    
* Increased emotional stability.     
* Recurring dreams involving white flowers.    
* Desire to sit beside the coffin.     

Psychological evaluations have not classified these effects as hostile.      

---     

# Threat Assessment     

LD-084 remains classified **Class II** because:    

* She is almost entirely passive.    
* Rarely leaves containment voluntarily.    
* Never attacks first.     
* Is easily contained while left undisturbed.    

However, provoking either the woman or the coffin has repeatedly resulted in catastrophic casualties.

### Research Note    

Several senior researchers have proposed that LD-084 is not an executioner...   

...but rather a caretaker.      

Whether she offers mercy or judgment appears to depend entirely on the life lived by the individual lying within her coffin.
