# File: LD-077 — "The White Maiden"   

**Designation:** Benevolent Humanoid Entity   

**Threat Level:** Class II   

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+   

**Containment Status:** Voluntary Cooperation   




![redacted](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HLSDJxoWkAAD3qz?format=jpg&name=large)





---   

## Overview   

LD-077, designated **"The White Maiden,"** is a humanoid female anomaly appearing approximately 20–25 years of age. The entity possesses long white hair, pale skin, and is consistently observed wearing a white dress of unknown material.   

Unlike the vast majority of recorded anomalies, LD-077 displays no hostility toward humanity and has repeatedly assisted DIVIDE personnel during medical emergencies.   

The anomaly possesses what is believed to be the most powerful healing ability currently documented.   

However, the healing can only be performed on individuals whom LD-077 personally judges to be **"pure."**   

The criteria used for this judgment remain completely unknown.   

---   

## Physical Description   

LD-077 appears physically fragile and displays no enhanced strength, speed, or durability.   

Medical scans indicate a body structure nearly identical to a human female.    

Despite this, direct interaction with the anomaly is heavily restricted due to its defensive abilities.   

---   

## Anomalous Properties   

### Divine Restoration   

LD-077 is capable of healing:   

* Physical injuries  
* Disease   
* Organ failure   
* Genetic abnormalities    
* Certain anomalous afflictions   

Healing is performed through direct touch.   

The process is painless and often instantaneous.   

Multiple terminally injured personnel have recovered fully within seconds.   

---   

### Judgment of Purity   

The anomaly refuses to heal every individual.   

Instead, LD-077 appears to evaluate a subject according to unknown standards.   

Requests for explanation have always resulted in the same response:   

> "They are not fit to be healed."   

Notably, the entity has:   

* Healed convicted criminals.   
* Refused decorated soldiers.   
* Refused researchers.   
* Refused children.   

No discernible pattern has been discovered.   

---   

### Golden Sanctuary   

When threatened, a golden spherical barrier manifests around LD-077.   

The barrier has never been successfully breached.   

The phenomenon appears completely automatic.   

The entity does not appear to consciously control it.    

---   

### The Golden Gaze   

When a man comes close to her they will start to lose their mind  

Upon crossing this threshold, the subject experiences an event designated:   

## The Golden Gaze   

Symptoms include:     

* Overwhelming fear.   
* Emotional collapse.   
* Immediate retreat behavior.   
* Severe psychological distress.   

Affected personnel often describe the sensation as being judged by something incomprehensibly ancient.   

---   

## APA Incident 077-03   

An APA operative with surgically suppressed emotional responses was instructed to approach LD-077.   

Upon reaching approximately one meter from the anomaly, the operative stopped.   

 he stated:   

> "For the first time in decades... I felt fear."   


---    

## Breach Resistance Test   

Following debates regarding the nature of LD-077's barrier, a controlled durability assessment was authorized.   

APA-01 was tasked with striking the Golden Sanctuary at maximum force.    

The resulting impact produced:    

* A continental-scale seismic event.   
* Widespread shockwaves.   
* Approximately 2 centimeters of measured continental displacement.   
* Earthquake activity lasting nearly 30 minutes.   

The barrier sustained only a single visible crack.   

Witnesses reported APA-01 immediately without permition to speak stating:   

> "I can break it in four more hits."   

Lead researchers terminated the test before continuation.   

The crack sealed itself several hours later.   

---     

## Relationship With The Golden-Eyed Lord   

The only known request ever made by LD-077 concerns the entity designated:   

**"The Golden-Eyed Lord"**   

Whenever the subject is mentioned, LD-077 displays visible distress.    

The anomaly has repeatedly stated:   

> "Please keep him away from me."   

No further explanation has been provided.   

Due to the severity of her reaction, all interaction between the two entities has been permanently prohibited.   

---   

## Behavior   

LD-077 is polite, cooperative, and generally willing to assist DIVIDE personnel.   

She frequently spends time:   

* Reading books.   
* Walking containment gardens.   
* Speaking with medical staff.   

The anomaly has never attempted escape.   

She has never harmed another individual.   

Researchers note that despite her gentle nature, even senior personnel feel uncomfortable in her presence, often describing a sense that they are being silently evaluated.   

---   

## Threat Assessment   

LD-077 remains classified as **Class II** because:   

* She demonstrates virtually no offensive capability.   
* She cooperates willingly.   
* She poses little direct danger.   

However, researchers acknowledge that the true extent of her defensive capabilities remains unknown.
