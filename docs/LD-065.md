# File: LD-065 — “The Rabbit Mother”  

**Designation:** Humanoid Swarm Controller  
**Threat Level:** Class II  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+  
**Containment Status:** Uncontained — Active Monitoring Program  

---  

## Overview  

LD-065, designated **“The Rabbit Mother,”** is a humanoid anomaly appearing as a young female estimated to be between 16–20 years of age.  

The entity stands approximately:  

* 165 cm tall  
* 48 kg in weight  

LD-065 is almost always observed wearing:  

* A white rabbit mask  
* A worn black-and-white dress  
* Pink ribbon-like arm coverings  

The anomaly is typically accompanied by large numbers of ordinary rabbits designated:  

## LD-065-A  

Unlike many dangerous anomalies, LD-065 possesses no abnormal physical durability.  

Medical analysis of recovered biological samples suggests the entity's body is almost entirely human.  

The anomaly can be killed through conventional means, including:  

* Firearms  
* Blunt trauma  
* Blood loss  
* Explosive force  

Despite this vulnerability, LD-065 remains dangerous due to its ability to command and coordinate large rabbit populations over significant distances.  

---  

## Physical Description  

The rabbit mask worn by LD-065 has never been removed successfully.  

Attempts to forcibly remove it have resulted in extreme resistance from nearby LD-065-A instances.  

The mask itself appears fused to the anomaly's face through unknown means.  

Researchers remain uncertain whether:  

* The mask conceals a human face  
* The mask is the anomaly's actual face  
* The mask itself is anomalous  

No confirmed observations of the entity without the mask exist.  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-065 generally avoids direct contact with humans.  

During periods of inactivity, the anomaly has been observed:  

* Feeding rabbits  
* Constructing nests  
* Speaking to animals  
* Wandering forests and abandoned farmland  

The entity demonstrates childlike behavior for extended periods.  

However, this behavior changes dramatically when:  

* Hunger occurs  
* Rabbits are harmed  
* The entity perceives a threat  

During these periods, LD-065 enters a highly aggressive hunting state.  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Rabbit Command  

LD-065 can exert direct control over rabbits within a radius of approximately:  

## 10 kilometers  

Affected rabbits demonstrate:  

* Coordinated movement  
* Pack hunting behavior  
* Complex ambush tactics  
* Extreme obedience  

The rabbits appear capable of understanding instructions far beyond normal animal intelligence.  

---  

### Swarm Summoning  

When distressed or hungry, LD-065 emits an unknown signal that rapidly attracts nearby rabbits.  

Within hours, thousands of rabbits may gather around the anomaly.  

Large manifestations have exceeded:  

## 15,000 rabbits  

in a single location.  

---  

### Feeding Frenzy  

During hunting periods, LD-065 directs rabbit swarms toward selected targets.  

Affected rabbits display behavior completely inconsistent with their species.  

Observed actions include:  

* Surrounding victims  
* Biting exposed flesh  
* Damaging eyes and extremities  
* Preventing escape through sheer numbers  

While individual rabbits pose little threat, large swarms have caused numerous fatalities.  

---  

### Consumption Behavior  

After a successful hunt, LD-065 consumes portions of the victim's remains.  

The anomaly appears to require conventional food intake but demonstrates a strong preference for human flesh.  

This behavior is believed to be the primary motivation behind many attacks.  

---  

## Weaknesses  

Unlike most hostile anomalies, LD-065 is exceptionally fragile.  

The entity possesses:  

* No enhanced durability  
* No regenerative abilities  
* No resistance to conventional weapons  

A single well-placed gunshot is believed sufficient to neutralize the anomaly.  

Because of this, most encounters become dangerous only when large rabbit populations are already present.  

---  

## Incident Record — White Meadow Event  

The largest confirmed attack occurred near an isolated farming community.  

Residents initially reported:  

* Unusually large rabbit populations  
* Missing livestock  
* Strange sightings of a masked girl  

Three days later:  

* Eleven civilians disappeared  
* Hundreds of rabbits were found throughout the area  
* Large quantities of human remains were recovered  

A DIVIDE response team arrived after local authorities became overwhelmed.  

LD-065 escaped before containment forces could establish a perimeter.  

---  

## Containment Attempts  

Multiple containment efforts have failed due to:  

* Constant movement  
* Remote habitat selection  
* Assistance from rabbit populations  

The anomaly frequently relocates before containment teams arrive.  

Current strategy focuses on:  

* Tracking abnormal rabbit migrations  
* Monitoring rural disappearances  
* Maintaining rapid response teams  

---  

## Containment Protocols  

If LD-065 is located:  

1. Establish a perimeter outside rabbit control range.  
2. Eliminate swarm concentrations before engagement.  
3. Prioritize direct capture of LD-065.  
4. Avoid prolonged operations allowing additional rabbits to gather.  
5. Prevent civilian contact with the anomaly.  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-065 is classified as:  

# **Class II**  

Reasoning:  

* High lethality under specific conditions  
* Capable of coordinating massive animal swarms  
* Difficult to locate  

However:  

* Extremely vulnerable physically  
* No large-scale destructive capability  
* Easily neutralized if isolated  

Researchers agree that LD-065's threat comes almost entirely from the creatures it commands rather than the anomaly itself.
