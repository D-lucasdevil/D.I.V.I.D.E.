# File: LD-055 — “The Drowned Shepherd”   

**Designation:** Maritime/Parasitic Humanoid Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** Class III  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+  
**Containment Status:** Deep-Sea Observation Containment Active

---  

## Overview  

LD-055, designated **“The Drowned Shepherd,”** is a partially humanoid deep-ocean anomaly associated with disappearances near coastal fog zones, abandoned fishing routes, and flooded settlements.  

The entity resembles a tall emaciated humanoid draped in heavily deteriorated black maritime cloth. The upper torso appears skeletal and elongated, while the lower body transitions into multiple cephalopod-like appendages of unknown biological origin.  

Its most recognizable feature is an oversized vertical jaw structure lined with multiple rows of hooked teeth extending far beyond normal anatomical limitations.  

The anomaly carries a rusted shepherd’s hook-like staff believed to function as both a weapon and navigational tool.  

Despite its appearance, LD-055 moves with extreme silence.  

Witnesses frequently report:  

* No audible footsteps  
* No water displacement sounds  
* Sudden appearance within heavy fog  
* Sensation of being “guided” before disappearance events  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-055 displays unusually passive behavior unless individuals attempt to flee its presence.  

The entity has been observed:  

* Slowly approaching isolated individuals  
* Standing motionless near shorelines for extended periods  
* Watching vessels from submerged positions beneath the waterline  
* Mimicking distorted human breathing sounds  

Unlike most predatory anomalies, LD-055 rarely attacks immediately.  

Research suggests the anomaly prefers:  

* Psychological destabilization  
* Isolation of targets  
* Gradual exhaustion and panic induction  

Survivors describe overwhelming compulsions to:  

* Follow the entity into fog-covered areas  
* Enter deep water voluntarily  
* Ignore nearby rescue attempts  

The anomaly appears especially active during:  

* Heavy storms  
* Dense coastal fog  
* Lunar tidal peaks  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Fog Manifestation  

LD-055 can generate dense abnormal fog capable of spreading across large coastal areas.  

Effects include:  

* Severe visibility reduction  
* Auditory distortion  
* Compass malfunction  
* Radio interference  

Personnel operating within affected zones report difficulty determining direction even with GPS assistance.  

---  

### Vocal Luring  

The entity emits low-frequency vocalizations mixed with distorted human whispers.  

Observed effects:  

* Auditory hallucinations  
* Temporary memory disruption  
* Compulsive attraction toward water sources  
* Panic attacks in prolonged exposure  

Certain victims report hearing:  

* Deceased relatives  
* Missing crew members  
* Personal cries for help  

No recordings successfully preserve the voices without corruption.  

---  

### Tentacle Assimilation  

The lower appendages of LD-055 are capable of extending through water at abnormal speeds.  

Functions include:  

* Silent drowning of targets  
* Rapid underwater movement  
* Dragging victims beneath structures or vessels  

Recovered bodies often display:  

* Water-filled lungs despite no drowning conditions  
* Circular suction trauma  
* Severe spinal distortion  

---  

### Feeding State  

After consuming multiple victims, LD-055 enters a temporary escalation phase.  

During this state:  

* Fog density increases dramatically  
* Appendage length expands  
* The jaw structure partially unfolds vertically  

Witnesses describe the entity during this phase as:  

> “Too large to belong in shallow water.”  

---  

## Weakness  

LD-055 demonstrates strong environmental dependency.  

The anomaly weakens significantly when:  

* Removed from humid environments  
* Exposed to sustained high-intensity heat  
* Isolated from large water sources  

Testing confirms the entity becomes:  

* Slower  
* Less regenerative  
* Unable to manifest dense fog efficiently  

Salt dehydration weapon systems showed partial effectiveness during containment trials.  

---  

## Incident Record — Black Tide Event  

The largest confirmed manifestation occurred near a collapsed fishing settlement following a severe storm system.  

Initial reports described:  

* Entire docks covered in unnatural fog  
* Missing rescue teams  
* Emergency broadcasts containing distorted breathing noises  

Confirmed losses:  

* 63 civilians
* 14 coast guard personnel  
* 2 DIVIDE retrieval units  

Recovered vessels were found:  

* Completely abandoned  
* Flooded internally despite calm seas  
* Covered in unidentified organic residue  

---  

## Containment Operation  

Containment was achieved after DIVIDE maritime units deployed:  

* Thermal bombardment systems  
* Offshore dehydration emitters  
* Electrified containment nets  

During the operation:  

* Multiple appendages were severed  
* Fog concentration temporarily dispersed  
* LD-055 retreated into an offshore trench zone  

A reinforced abyssal containment perimeter was later established around the trench.  

Direct underwater retrieval remains prohibited.  

---  

## Containment Protocols  

LD-055 is monitored within:  

* A restricted deep-sea containment exclusion zone  

Containment requirements:  

* No civilian maritime travel permitted nearby  
* Continuous sonar surveillance active  
* Thermal suppression arrays maintained at all times  
* Fog density fluctuations monitored hourly  

Emergency protocol:  

If unexplained fog spreads beyond:  

* 3 kilometers from containment zone  

Then:  

* Coastal evacuation procedures initiated  
* Maritime lockdown authorized  
* SORAD aquatic response teams deployed immediately  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-055 is classified as:  

## **Class III**  

Reasoning:  

* Dangerous localized lethality  
* Strong environmental manipulation  
* Effective psychological warfare capabilities  
* Maritime ambush specialization  

However:  

* Limited effectiveness outside aquatic regions  
* Escalation remains temporary  
* Large-scale extinction potential currently unlikely  

Under controlled conditions, elite DIVIDE units can suppress the anomaly successfully.
