# File: LD-085 — "Eclipse"    

**Designation:** Anomalous Tactical Dagger    
 
**Threat Level:** Class II

**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Level 4+   

**Containment Status:** Active Service — PHANTOM Armory    









---    

## Overview    

LD-085, designated **"Eclipse,"** Eclipse emphasizes stealth, mobility, and precision over raw destructive power.   

The weapon resembles a matte-black tactical combat dagger measuring approximately **38 centimeters** in total length. Embedded within the pommel is a polished **black crystalline core**, while a second **amber-orange crystal** is integrated into the forward section of the handle where the wielder's index finger naturally rests when holding the blade in a reverse grip.     

Each crystal activates a separate anomalous ability.    

---    

# Physical Description    

The blade is forged from an unidentified black alloy possessing exceptional durability while remaining surprisingly lightweight.    

Unlike conventional metal, the edge absorbs light rather than reflecting it, making the weapon extremely difficult to detect under low-light conditions.     

Despite repeated testing, the blade has never required sharpening.      

---     

# Ability I — Shadow Anchor    

The black crystal located at the pommel activates Eclipse's primary mobility function.     

Upon pressing the crystal, the user's shadow detaches from their feet and becomes an autonomous, humanoid silhouette.     

The shadow:        

* Cannot Move Separately  
* Cannot attack or interact with objects.    
* Is completely intangible.     
* Is visible only under direct lighting.     

Once deployed, the user has approximately **150 meters** of operational range.      

At any moment before the limit expires, pressing the crystal again instantly exchanges the user's position with the shadow.     

Teleportation occurs without sound, momentum loss, or visible energy release.     
 
If the shadow exceeds 150 meters or remains active longer than five minutes, it dissipates and the crystal enters a recharge cycle lasting approximately sixty seconds.     

---    

# Ability II — Severance Array     

When Eclipse is held in a reverse grip, the wielder gains access to the amber crystal.     

Activating the crystal releases eleven compressed anomalous cutting arcs directly ahead of the user in a fan-shaped formation.      

Each slash exists for less than one-tenth of a second before disappearing.    

Capabilities include:      

* Effortlessly slicing reinforced concrete.    
* Severing armored vehicles with repeated use.     
* Cutting through steel bulkheads.    
* Neutralizing multiple hostile targets simultaneously.    

The attack extends approximately 1 meter.    

Each activation consumes significant anomalous energy.    

Following use, the amber crystal requires approximately fifteen seconds before reactivation.    

---   

# Limitations    

Despite its destructive appearance, Eclipse possesses several important limitations.    

The Severance Array cannot penetrate:    

* Class IV anomaly-grade alloys.   
* Certain containment composites.   
* High-density anomalous barriers.    
* Divine-grade or celestial anomalous materials.    

Likewise, Shadow Anchor cannot teleport through specially designed anti-spatial containment fields installed throughout DIVIDE blacksites.    

---    

# Tactical Usage    

PHANTOM operators commonly employ Eclipse to:     

* Bypass security checkpoints.    
* Escape ambushes.    
* Eliminate multiple targets in confined spaces.    
* Conduct silent assassinations.    
* Perform rapid extraction missions.    

The weapon has become one of PHANTOM's most valued assets due to its reliability and versatility.    

---     



# Known Users    

Only specially trained PHANTOM operators are authorized to wield Eclipse.    

Improper activation frequently results in users teleporting into hazardous environments due to poor shadow placement.    

Because of this, mastery requires months of training.
