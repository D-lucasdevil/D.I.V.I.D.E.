**File: LD-048 — “Price of Immortality”**  

**Designation: Dual Anomaly System**  

**LD-048A: Humanoid Entity**  
**LD-048B: Regenerative Artifact**  

**Threat Level:**  

**D-048A: Class II (Non-aggressive but hazardous)**  
**LD-048B: Class III (Lethal misuse potential)**  

**Clearance Required: DIVIDE Level 2–5 (Dependent on interaction type)**   
**Containment Status: Restrained / Heavily Monitored**

**Overview:**  

**LD-048 consists of two interconnected anomalies:**  

LD-048A — an indestructible humanoid entity  
LD-048B — an embedded artifact responsible for anomalous regenerative effects  

Both components are permanently linked. Separation has proven impossible.  

LD-048A — Humanoid Entity  
Description:  

LD-048A is a humanoid figure entirely covered in black crystalline structures emitting a faint blood-red glow.  

**The entity:**  

Does not move  
Does not react to stimuli  
Shows no signs of awareness or biological function  
Anomalous Properties:  
1. Visual Rejection Effect  
Any attempt to:  
Record  
Photograph  
Digitally scan  

**Results in:**  

Immediate destruction or malfunction of the device  

**If viewed directly:**  

Human observers experience delayed-onset blindness  
Short observation window allows partial description before effect triggers  
2. Damage Absorption  
LD-048A absorbs all forms of damage:  
Ballistic  
Explosive  
Thermal  
Anomalous  
Absorbed energy is believed to be converted into LD-048B crystalline material  

Upper limit: Unknown  

3. Signal Emission  

Upon receiving damage, LD-048A emits an anomalous radio signal.  

**Decoded message:**  

“%#$, I will find you and make you regret everything you’ve ever done to me.”  

Origin and meaning: Unknown  

LD-048B — Embedded Artifact  
**Description:**  

LD-048B is a crystalline structure embedded through the chest of LD-048A, positioned where a human heart would typically be located.  

Its form resembles a religious crest or cross-like symbol of unknown origin.  

**Separation Attempts:**  
Full removal:  
Results in immediate fragmentation  
Artifact regenerates inside LD-048A  
Partial fragments:  
Remain stable when separated  
Retain anomalous properties  
Primary Ability — “Conditional Resurrection”  

**When a fragment of LD-048B is:**  

Consumed  
or  
Embedded into a subject’s body  

The subject gains a 60-second anomalous state:  

**During the 60 Seconds:**  
No immediate visible changes  
Subject remains fully vulnerable  
If the Subject Dies Within 60 Seconds:  
Instant resurrection  
Full physical restoration  
LD-048B fragment dissolves completely  

**Limitations:**  

Does not restore:  
Blindness caused by LD-048A  
Old injuries  
Genetic damage or mutations  
If the Subject Survives Beyond 60 Seconds:  
LD-048B begins host conversion process:  

**Effects:**  

Crystalline growth spreads across the body  
Structures resemble LD-048B formations  
Full body crystallization occurs  

**Outcome:**  

Death  
Containment Protocol:  
LD-048A is to remain in a sealed observation chamber  
Direct visual exposure minimized  
All monitoring conducted via indirect or shielded systems  
LD-048B fragments:  
Stored separately  
Use restricted to authorized testing only  
Threat Assessment:  
LD-048A:  
Low mobility, but highly hazardous to perception  
Indestructible nature presents containment challenge  
LD-048B:  
High-risk artifact due to:  
Resurrection capability  
Fatal misuse condition  

Combined classification justifies elevated monitoring priority  

**Internal Memorandum:**  

“It doesn’t give immortality.  
It gives you one chance to deserve it.”  

— DIVIDE Anomalous Biology Division
