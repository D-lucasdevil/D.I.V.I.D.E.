# File: LD-066 — “The Silent Executioner”  

**Designation:** Humanoid Conceptual Anomaly  
**Threat Level:** **REDACTED**  
**Clearance Required:** DIVIDE Director Access Only  
**Containment Status:** Cooperative Containment   

---  

## Overview  

LD-066, designated **"The Silent Executioner,"** is a humanoid anomaly resembling a pale female standing approximately 1.8 meters in height.  

The entity possesses:  

* White hair  
* Golden eyes  
* Black ceremonial robes  
* Numerous floating scissor-like constructs surrounding her body  

The anomaly communicates exclusively in Latin and has demonstrated complete understanding of all known human languages despite never speaking them.  

Unlike most high-risk anomalies, LD-066 displays calm, polite, and cooperative behavior.  

Researchers universally agree that this demeanor is the only reason the anomaly remains under containment.  

Current threat classification remains:  

# REDACTED  

due to the complete inability of researchers to establish the upper limits of the entity's capabilities.  

---  

## Physical Description  

LD-066 appears biologically human.  

Medical scans reveal:  

* No detectable internal organs  
* No measurable heartbeat  
* No respiration  
* No signs of aging  

Despite this, the anomaly remains fully functional.  

Attempts to collect biological samples have failed.  

Any instrument attempting to remove tissue from the entity is severed before contact is achieved.  

Researchers remain uncertain whether LD-066 possesses a true physical body.  

---  

## Behavior  

LD-066 is generally cooperative and respectful toward facility personnel.  

The anomaly frequently engages in conversation and willingly answers questions.  

However, it refuses to discuss:  

* Its origin  
* Its purpose  
* The nature of its abilities  
* The identity of its targets  

When questioned regarding these topics, the entity commonly responds:  

> "Non est vestrum scire."  

Translation:  

> "It is not yours to know."  

The anomaly has never displayed unprovoked hostility.  

However, all attempts at forceful containment or termination have ended catastrophically.  

---  

## Primary Abilities  

### Scissor Manifestation  

LD-066 can summon anomalous scissors of varying size and composition.  

Manifestations range from:  

* 3 centimeters  
* To over 75 meters  

The scissors may appear:  

* In open air  
* Underground  
* Within structures  
* Directly adjacent to targets  

The largest confirmed manifestation bisected an entire mountain ridge during a containment incident.  

Material analysis has proven impossible.  

All manifested scissors disappear shortly after use.  

---  

### Invisible Constructs  

Most scissor manifestations are completely invisible.  

Victims often display injuries before realizing they have been attacked.  

Documented effects include:  

* Limb severance  
* Structural collapse  
* Precision dismemberment  
* Complete bisection  

Advanced sensors consistently fail to detect the constructs.  

---  

### Spatial Cutting  

LD-066 appears capable of cutting more than physical matter.  

Documented examples include:  

* Severing containment barriers  
* Splitting reinforced bunkers  
* Dividing moving vehicles  
* Creating temporary openings through solid structures  

Researchers believe the anomaly is not cutting objects themselves but rather the space occupied by those objects.  

This theory remains unconfirmed.  

---  

### Mouthed Manifestations  

LD-066 can generate large disembodied mouths designated:  

## LD-066-A  

The mouths may appear:  

* In midair  
* On surfaces  
* Underground  
* Within enclosed spaces  

Manifestation range appears limited to approximately:  

## 15 kilometers  

or any distance at which the anomaly can clearly perceive a target.  

The mouths possess:  

* Massive crushing force  
* Extreme durability  
* Rapid consumption capabilities  

Entire vehicles have disappeared into manifestations within seconds.  

---  

### Immortality  

No successful method of harming LD-066 has been discovered.  

Observed attempts include:  

* Firearms  
* Explosives  
* Anti-anomaly weaponry  
* Reality destabilization devices  

All efforts failed.  

The anomaly either avoided damage entirely or was completely left unaffected  

Researchers currently consider LD-066 effectively immortal.  

---  

## Relationship With LD-044  

One of the most unusual aspects of LD-066 is its interaction with:  

## LD-044 — "The Veiled Judgment"  

Despite displaying no fear toward any other anomaly, LD-066 refuses to approach LD-044 whenever possible.  

During the first recorded encounter, LD-066 immediately lowered her head in a bow.  

Security footage recorded the motion occurring so rapidly that a pressure wave was generated.  

The resulting shockwave:  

* Killed four security personnel  
* Destroyed nearby equipment  
* Shattered reinforced glass panels  

LD-066 remained in the bowed position until removed from the area.  

When questioned regarding the incident, she responded:  

> "Iudicium supra me stat."  

Translation:  

> "Judgment stands above me."  

Researchers have never successfully obtained further explanation.  

---  

## The Excursions  

LD-066 periodically requests permission to leave containment.  

A typical request consists of:  

> "Officium habeo."  

Translation:  

> "I have work to do."  

Early containment policy required denying these requests.  

This policy was abandoned following Incident 066-Alpha. 

---  

## Incident 066-Alpha  

Upon being denied permission to leave containment, LD-066 reportedly replied:  

> "Ah. Si ita dicitis."  

Translation:  

> "Ah. If you say so."  

The anomaly then manifested multiple large-scale scissors.  

The resulting event caused:  

* Severe facility destruction  
* Multiple containment breaches  
* Significant personnel casualties  

Security footage indicates LD-066 ignored all non-hostile personnel.  

Individuals who attacked the anomaly were immediately killed.  

Those who retreated or remained passive were left unharmed.  

After leaving the facility, LD-066 traveled at extreme speed toward an unknown individual.  

Upon arrival, witnesses reported the anomaly speaking:  

> "Mors est verus deus."  

Translation:  

> "Death is the true god."  

An LD-066-A manifestation subsequently consumed the target.  

LD-066 then calmly returned to the facility.  

No further aggression occurred.  

---  

## Current Containment Procedures  

Due to repeated failures of traditional containment methods, LD-066 is currently maintained under:  

## Cooperative Containment Doctrine  

Containment requirements:  

* Living quarters remain unlocked  
* No termination attempts authorized  
* No forced restraint permitted  
* Personnel are prohibited from obstructing excursions  

If LD-066 requests departure:  

* Permission is to be granted immediately  
* Destination tracking initiated  
* Maximum alert status activated  

Researchers have determined that attempting to prevent movement presents a substantially greater threat than allowing it.  

---  

## Threat Assessment  

LD-066 is classified as:  

# REDACTED  

Reasoning:  

* Potentially limitless offensive capability  
* Unknown upper power threshold  
* Effective immortality  
* Impossible termination prospects  
* Ability to bypass most conventional containment methods  

However:  

* Highly cooperative  
* Rarely hostile  
* Does not seek destruction  
* Consistently returns following excursions  

The anomaly's true danger remains impossible to quantify.
